1. six-family apartment building： 六户人家居住的公寓楼房

2. generally speaking： 一般意义上说
  Generally speaking, it's quite a fair settlement.
  大致上说，这样解决很恰当。

3. bring over： 带过来（本课中的意思）；使信服，使同意
  I told him to bring her over here today.
  我让他今天就带她到这儿来。
  You cannot bring me over by such an argument.
  你这样的论点不能改变我的看法。

4. in return： 作为报答
  I invited him to dinner in return for his help.
  我邀请他来吃饭以酬谢他的帮助。

5. a problem to your family： 你们家的一个问题
  a problem on the environmental protection：关于环境保护的问题
  the problem of how to move the heavy machinery：怎样移动这台沉重机器的问题
  a problem with the car：汽车的一个故障

6. as a result： 其结果是
  I got up late. As a result, I missed my train.
  我起晚了，结果没赶上火车。
  The flight was delayed as a result of fog.
  因有雾该航班误点。

7. freeze up： （使）冻结
  Our water pipes freeze up in such low temperatures.
  温度这么低，我们的水管子都冻了。
  The window has frozen up and I can't open it.
  窗户给冻上了，我打不开。

8. treat somebody with respect： 对某人带有敬意
  It was the first time I had been treated with such respect.
  我是第一次受到这样的礼遇。
  I have the greatest respect for him.
  我非常尊敬他。
  Out of respect, I took off my hat.
  我脱帽以示敬意。

9. have / make complaints about： 对……有抱怨，不满意（本课中的意思）；对……提出控诉
  I have a lot of complaints about the hotel room you've given me.
  我对你给我的旅馆房间很不满意。
  They had made complaints of bad workmanship to us.
  他们已经向我们投诉产品工艺低劣。

10. carry on： [口]吵吵闹闹，举止失常
  She does carry on, doesn't she?
  她真有点儿疯疯癫癫的，是吧？

11. have a word with somebody： （私下）与某人谈话
  Can I have a word with you before you go to the meeting?
  你去开会之前，我能私下和你说句话吗？

12. apart from： 除此之外
  Apart from his teeth, he is quite good-looking.
  除了牙齿之外，他还是挺帅的。
  Apart from being too large, the hat doesn't suit me.
  这顶帽子不仅太大，而且我戴着也不合适。

13. I don't think much of the restaurant.
  我觉得餐厅不怎么样。
  He's not much of a father.
  他算不上好父亲。
