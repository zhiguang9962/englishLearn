1. be good for： 对……有益
  Is this kind of food good for him?
  这种食物对他身体有益吗？
  Sunshine is good for your plants.
  你的花草要多晒太阳。

2. have fun with ...： 与……嬉戏，玩……很开心
  The children are having a lot of fun with the building blocks.
  孩子们玩积木正玩得很开心。
  We had lots of fun at the fair yesterday.
  昨天我们在游乐场上玩得很高兴。

3. be right for： 适合于……
  Their ages were just right for a match.
  他俩的年龄正好相配。
  That coat is just right for you.
  那件大衣你穿正合适。

4. ride the waves： 乘浪（前进）
  ride a bicycle：骑自行车
  ride a horse：骑马
  Hearing the news, I immediately rode home on my bicycle.
  听了这消息，我立即骑自行车赶回家去。

5. change with the season： 随季节变化而变化
  You should change your clothes with the weather.
  你应该随天气的变化而换衣服。
  I wouldn't change places with him.
  我不愿与他换位置。
  We are sure we can change the looks of our city with our own hands.
  我们相信我们一定能用自己的双手改变我们城市的面貌。

6. believe it or not： 信不信由你
  Believe it or not, this mosque is in the heart of London.
  信不信由你，这座清真寺就在伦敦的市中心。

7. have this advantage over ordinary people： 在这一点上胜过一般的人
  Her French upbringing gives her certain advantages over other students in her class.
  她的法国式教养使她比班上其他同学略胜一筹。

8. at least： 至少；无论如何
  She drank at least half a bottle of whisky a day.
  她每天至少喝半瓶威士忌。
  She may be slow but at least she's reliable.
  她可能是迟钝一些，但至少很可靠。

9. experiment on： 对……进行实验
  He is experimenting on a white mouse.
  他正在用一只白鼠做实验。
  He experimented with a new method of teaching English and succeeded.
  他实验了新的英语教学法，并取得了成功。
