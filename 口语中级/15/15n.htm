1. When memory began for me, ...： 我刚记事时，……
  It happened in 1911, if my memory serves me right.
  如果我没记错的话，这事发生在1911年。
  "Even memory is not necessary for love." (Thornton Wilder)
  “爱甚至不需要记忆。”（桑顿・瓦尔德）

2. a straight nose which ended in a slightly flattened tip： 直鼻梁，鼻尖稍稍有点塌
  After full 12 months the Long March ended in triumph.
  经过了整整12个月，长征胜利结束了。
  A people's just war is bound to end in victory.
  人民的正义战争最后一定会胜利。
  Our match ended in a draw.
  我们的比赛打成了平局。

3. at the corners of his eyes： 他的眼角上
  I saw her coming from the corner of my eye.
  我用眼角的余光一瞟，见她来了。
  He went out with a cigarette in the corner of his mouth.
  他嘴角叼了一支烟走了出去。

4. at the end of： 在……结尾，在……末端
  I live in a house at the end of the street.
  我住在街道尽头的那栋房子里。
  At the end of last month he came to visit me.
  上个月末，他来看我了。
  I've been looking after four young children all day and I really am at the end of my tether!
  我整天都在照料四个小孩，确实已筋疲力尽了！

5. pick up： 捡起（本课中的意思）；获得；（顺便）买，（顺便用车）来接；加速；好起来
  I cautiously picked up the broken glasses.
  我小心地把（摔）坏了的眼镜捡了起来。
  Where did he pick up his technical skill?
  他的技术是在哪儿学的？
  I'll pick you up at your office.
  我到你办公室来接你。
  Output picked up again in the following year.
  第二年的产量又开始上升了。
  She is picking up wonderfully since she came out of hospital.
  她出院后恢复得非常好。

6. have a desire to do something： 想要做某事
  They showed a desire to improve relations.
  他们表现出改善关系的愿望。
  He has a great desire to go to college.
  他有强烈的上大学的欲望。

7. have a deep love of beauty： 深爱美丽的事物
  a love of freedom：对自由的热爱
  love for a child：对孩子的爱

8. be of value (= be valuable)： 有价值
  be of importance (= be important)：重要

9. earn a living (= make a living)： 谋生
  scrounge a living：靠乞讨过日子
  eke out a living：勉强维持生活
  plain living：俭朴的生活
  high living：奢侈舒适的生活

10. two hours of solitude： 独处两小时

11. a creature of habit： 生活极有规律的人

12. an infinite variety in his pursuits： 他有着各种各样的追求
  a life full of variety：丰富多彩的生活
  fresh varieties in season：各色时鲜货
  a wide variety of occupations：各行各业

13. a circle of friends： 一群朋友
  my circle of friends：我的朋友圈子
  the family circle：家庭圈子
  in theatrical circles：在戏剧界

14. in a sense： 在某种意义上
  I think he may be right in a sense.
  我想，在某种意义上说，他是对的。
  Your mind was in no sense brilliant.
  你的头脑一点也算不上聪明。
  The election was in every sense unfair.
  这次选举不管从什么意义上说都是不公平的。

15. at first glance： 乍看
  At first glance the plan seemed unworkable.
  乍一看此计划好像不可行。
  He saw at a glance that she was coming.
  他一眼就看到她来了。
  I used to take a glance at the newspaper before reading.
  在看报之前，我习惯浏览一下标题。

16. take somebody for ...： 把某人当成……
  At first they took her for a foreigner.
  一开始他们都把她当成了外国人。
  What did you take me for?
  你把我当成什么了？

17. be fussy about： 对……很讲究，很挑剔
  He is fussy about food.
  他挑食。
  She is always fussy about her personal appearance.
  她总是过于讲究外表。

18. be reflected in： 反映在……
  This shift in the balance of forces is also reflected in international finance.
  力量对比的变化也在国际金融中得到反映。
  This change of outlook was immediately reflected in his works.
  观点的改变立即在他的作品中得到反映。
