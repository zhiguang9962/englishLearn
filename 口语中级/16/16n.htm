1. be enthusiastic about： 对……充满热情
  He is enthusiastic about the plans he has made.
  他对自己订出的计划满怀热情。
  The retired worker is very enthusiastic about neighbourhood affairs.
  这个退休工人对邻里之间的事情非常热心。

2. make a list of： 列表
  You'd better make a list of things to buy.
  你最好列个购物单。

3. make a schedule of one's time： 做时间表
  a schedule of guided tours：导游时间表
  a schedule of postal charges：邮资价目表
  set up a schedule for the next two days：提出后两天的日程安排

4. fill in committed time： （在单子上）填好必须做的事情所占用的时间
  I can't come on Sunday ― I'm already committed.
  星期天我不能来――我有事。
  The regiment is already committed to the eastern front.
  该团承担东线的战斗任务。

5. decide on： 就……做出决定
  Let's put our heads together and decide on a plan of action.
  让咱们集思广益，决定一项行动计划。
  You'd better not decide on important matters too quickly.
  你最好不要对重要的事情匆忙地做出决定。

6. set aside： 拨出，留出（本课中的意思）；不理会，搁置
  She managed to set aside a large sum for the house.
  她设法拨出一大笔钱以供买房之用。
  He set all their offers aside.
  他们提出要帮忙，他没有理会。

7. happen to： （某人）发生了什么事，（某物）发生了什么情况
  Has anything happened to her?
  她出什么事了吗？
  What has happened to your recorder?
  你的录音机怎么了？

8. look around ... for： 环视……以寻找某物或某人
  He looked around the kitchen for a box of matches, but he couldn't find one.
  他在厨房到处找火柴，却没找着。
  I just want to look around and see if I can get any gifts to take home.
  我只想随便看看，看能不能买到什么礼品带回国去。

9. free of everything but study materials： 除了学习材料、用品以外，不放其它东西
  free of pollution：无污染
  Delivery is free of charge if goods are paid for in advance.
  如预付货款就可免费送货。

10. concentrate on： 集中，全神贯注于
  I can't concentrate on my studies with all that noise going on.
  吵闹声不绝于耳，我无法集中精力学习。
  We decided to concentrate on the Asian market this year.
  我们决定今年把工作重点集中在亚洲市场。

11. look over： 审阅，翻阅（本课中的意思）；打量；检查
  When I came in he was looking over my essay.
  我进来的时候，他正在翻看我写的文章。
  He asked my age, height, and weight and looked me over.
  他询问了我的年龄、身高和体重，又打量了我一番。
  I think this engine needs to be looked over.
  我看这部发动机需要检查一下。

12. make good use of： 充分利用
  We should make full use of our natural resources.
  我们应该充分利用自己的自然资源。
  You can make any use of it you like.
  你想怎样利用它都行。

13. take advantage of： 利用
  We should take full advantage of the exceptional opportunities open in exports.
  我们应该充分利用出口方面现成的难得良机。
  We had better take advantage of the warmer weather by going for a walk this afternoon.
  我们最好趁着今天下午天气比较暖和，出去散散步。

14. get off the subject： 离题
  I can't get the ink off my hand.
  我手上的墨水洗不掉。
  He won't get off so easily next time.
  下次可不能这样轻易地放过他。

15. go over： 审阅，检查，复习
  You need to go over the contract again and make sure everything is in order.
  你需要把合同再看一遍，确认是否一切都符合要求。
  I went over every process again, but without result.
  我把每道工序又检查了一遍，却仍没有结果。
  She is going over her notes before the exam.
  考试前她正在复习笔记。

16. the ideas that come into your mind： 你脑子里想到的各种想法
  new ideas will come to you：你还会想出新主意

17. slow down： （使）慢下来
  speed up：加速

18. lead to： 引起，导致
  This crisis led to the downfall of the then government.
  这一危机使当时的政府倒台了。
  To be blind to this will lead to the gravest error.
  看不到这一点，就会犯极大的错误。

19. a waste of time： 浪费时间
  a waste of talent：天赋的浪费
  a waste of waters：茫茫大海，一片汪洋
