1. juvenile delinquency： 青少年犯罪

2. cram with： 填满
  The boy crammed his mouth with food.
  那男孩往嘴里塞满了食物。
  You wrote an essay crammed with quotations.
  你写的文章引语连篇。

3. be popular with： 受……欢迎
  She is very popular with her boss.
  她很得老板赏识。
  These measures are popular with the electorate.
  这些措施受到选民的欢迎。

4. watch out： 当心，提防
  In those days you would easily be cheated if you didn't watch out.
  那时候，你要是不提防，很容易受骗。
  Watch out for the man behind you.
  当心你后面的那个人。

5. peer through： 透过……窥视
  peer through a gap：从缝隙中窥视
  peer out of the window：凝视窗外
  peer over the wall：隔墙看
  peer into the mist：向雾中张望

6. catch a glimpse of： 瞥见
  There I caught glimpses of young people working at the machines.
  在那儿我瞥见一些年轻人在机器前干活。
  I caught a glimpse of a friend at the end of the room.
  我看到在房间的尽头有位朋友。

7. be connected with： 与……有关系
  He is the only man connected with known criminals.
  他是与人所共知的罪犯惟一有关系的人。
  I heard that she was connected with a noble family.
  我听说她是某名门望族的亲戚。

8. in need： 在危难中
  A friend in need is a friend indeed.
  [谚]患难之交才是真正的朋友。
  The family is in dire need.
  这个家庭极为贫困。

9. commit a crime： 犯罪
  They committed many horrible crimes against the people.
  他们对人民犯下了许多可怕的罪行。
  If you don't understand this, you will commit the gravest mistakes.
  你如果不理解这一点，就会犯极大的错误。

10. be exposed to： 暴露于（本课中的意思）；遭受
  Now it has been exposed to the light of day.
  现在它已经暴露在光天化日之下了。
  They are exposed to attack everywhere.
  他们到处被动挨打。
