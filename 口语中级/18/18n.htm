1. keep fit： 保持健康
  He keeps himself fit by running 5 miles every day.
  为了保持身体健康，他每天跑5英里。
  She has been ill and isn't fit for work yet.
  她一直在生病，还不能工作。

2. cycle to work： 骑自行车上班
  drive to work：开车上班

3. have a lot of time to do something： 有大量时间做某事
  I'm sorry. I am too busy to have time to go to the cinema with you.
  对不起，我太忙了，没时间和你去看电影。
  Do you have time for dinner with me tonight?
  你今晚有时间和我一起吃晚饭吗？

4. take people out to lunch： 带人出去吃午饭
  take us to the Museum of Natural History：带我们去自然历史博物馆
  take the dog for a walk：带狗散步
  take me to my seat：带我找到座位

5. "Expense account" lunches： 费用账户午餐（饭费从公司账目上支付）

6. do a lot of tests： 做很多检查
  an eye test：眼睛检查
  a test for cancer：癌试验

7. take everything out of somebody： 夺走某人的一切
  The dentist took out two of my teeth.
  牙医拔掉了我两颗牙。
  I cannot take out the ink stains from my blouse.
  我衬衣上的墨水印洗不掉。

8. have a family to support： 需要养家
  I was supported by my parents when I was studying.
  我求学期间由父母供养。

9. put on： 穿上，戴上（本课中的意思）；上演；装出（某种样子）；打开（灯或收音机等）
  Put on your coat, or you will catch a cold.
  穿上大衣，否则你会感冒的。
  We put on a show last week.
  上星期我们进行了演出。
  Her modesty was all put on.
  她的谦虚都是装出来的。
  Would you mind me putting the radio on?
  你介意我打开收音机吗？

10. or rather： 或者说，说得更确切些
  We stayed at my friend's house, or rather at my friend's parents' house.
  我们住在我朋友家里，说得确切些是在我朋友的父母家。
  This medicine, or rather drug, has a violent effect.
  这种药品，或者说麻醉剂，有强烈的效果。

11. go cycling with me： 和我一起去骑车
  Many people went begging in past times.
  过去很多人讨饭。
  Don't you go looking for trouble?
  你这不是去自找麻烦吗？
