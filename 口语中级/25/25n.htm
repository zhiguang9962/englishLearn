1. at the terminus： 在（公共汽车）终点站
  an airline terminus：终点航空站
  How much to the terminus, please?
  请问，到终点站多少钱？

2. think to oneself： 心中想，盘算
  "She's not telling the truth," I thought to myself.
  “她没有讲实话，”我心中暗想。

3. have the intention of forming a line： 打算排队
  I have no intention of marrying yet.
  我还没有要结婚的打算。
  I told him of my intention to resign.
  我告诉他我打算辞职。

4. in the best strategic position： 处于最佳战略位置
  Each soldier got into position to defend the airfield.
  士兵们各就各位，保卫机场。

5. be over-optimistic about (doing) something： 对（做）某事过于乐观
  He remained optimistic about the meeting.
  他对会议依然抱乐观态度。
  I was over-optimistic about winning the competition.
  我对赢得比赛过于乐观了。

6. be terrified at： 因为……吓了一跳
  I am terrified at the prospect of being alone in the house.
  我要是自己待在一所房子里就很害怕。
  I was terrified out of my wit.
  我吓得魂不附体。

7. go into action： 投入战斗
  At daybreak the troops went into action.
  拂晓时部队投入战斗。

8. come to a halt： 停下来
  Work came to a halt when the machine broke down.
  机器出了毛病，工作便停了下来。

9. The scramble that followed defies description.
  随后抢着上车的混乱则无法描述。
  Alaska's scenic grandeur almost defies description.
  阿拉斯加的景色之壮丽几乎无法描绘。
  These phenomena seemingly defy rational explanation.
  这些现象似乎不能得到合理的解释。

10. elbow one's way forward： 用肘推挤着向前走
  I elbowed my way through the crowd.
  我挤过人群。
  She elbows her way into the best circles.
  她设法挤进了上层社会的圈子。

11. a block of solid wall： 一堵坚实的墙
  a block sum：大量金额
  a block in traffic：交通阻塞

12. an eternity of pushing and shouting： 无休止的推搡和嚷嚷
  an eternity of waiting：漫漫苦等

13. have the decency to give up his seat to Granny： 能讲公德，给奶奶让座
  Only doctors and nurses have the decency to wear masks.
  只有医生和护士戴口罩才得体。
  She had the decency to admit that it was her fault.
  她还算有礼貌，承认这是她的错。

14. be glued to their seats： 粘在了座位上
  Can you glue the wood to the metal?
  你能把木头粘到金属上吗？
  He's glued to the television.
  他目不转睛地盯着电视。

15. wobble on one's feet： 两腿哆嗦
  Her voice sometimes wobbles on high notes.
  她唱高音时有时声音发颤。
  I have to be on my feet all day, going from one machine to the other.
  我得整天脚不停步，从一台机器走到另一台。

16. doze off： 打盹
  He dozed off in the middle of the Minister's speech.
  部长讲话时，他打了一会儿盹。

17. be concerned with： 对……表示关心
  He is very concerned with the people's well-being.
  他很关心群众生活。
  I am rather concerned about your health.
  我相当担心你的健康。

18. make it： 成功，使……实现
  Let's have a try. I believe we will make it.
  试一试吧。我相信我们会成功的。

19. work out： 产生结果，成功（本课中的意思）；想出；解决；使筋疲力尽；算出；弄懂
  Things worked out quite differently.
  结果大不相同。
  We worked out our own road to socialism.
  我们开辟了一条有自己特色的通向社会主义的道路。
  Can you work out on the map where we are now?
  你能在地图上找到我们现在所在的位置吗？
  I cannot work out what he meant by "no hope".
  我无法理解他说的“没有希望”是什么意思。

20. Highland Park： 芝加哥一个中、上阶层人居住的郊区
  Michigan Avenue：密歇根大街（芝加哥的一条繁华街道）
