1. a firm believer in equality and freedom： 坚信平等自由的人
  a believer in Christianity：基督教信徒

2. be entitled to the same rights and opportunities： 被赋予同等权利和机会
  Only you were entitled to see the documents.
  只有你才有权看这些文件。
  I had a pass that entitled me to free travel on the railway.
  我有一张可以免费乘火车的证件。

3. wait hand and foot on somebody： 从头到脚地伺候某人
  A Japanese wife was formerly expected to wait upon her husband hand and foot.
  从前，日本妇女非得无微不至地伺候丈夫不可。

4. by mistake： 错误地
  I used his glass by mistake.
  我误用了他的杯子。
  I believe my account was suspended by mistake. Please resume it.
  我想我的账户被错误地暂停服务了。请将其恢复。

5. be content with： 满足于……
  I am very content with my life at present.
  我对目前的生活心满意足。
  She is content with very little.
  她易于满足。

6. be capable of doing something： 有能力做某事
  Nobody is capable of climbing that tree.
  没有人能爬上那棵树。
  She is capable of judging art.
  她有艺术鉴赏力。

7. to one's delight： 使某人高兴的是……
  To the teacher's great delight, all her students passed the examination.
  使老师极为高兴的是她的学生全部通过了考试。

8. adapt to： 适应，适合
  You must adapt your thinking and your work to the new circumstances.
  你必须使自己的思想和工作适应新的情况。
  I don't think I shall ever adapt myself to this hot weather.
  我想无论如何我也适应不了这样炎热的天气。

9. take leave of one's senses： 发疯
  Those students who want to complete a period of work experience will be permitted to take leave of absence for one academic year.
  那些想有一段工作经历的学生可以获准离开一学年。

10. be clogged with： 被……填满，因……而阻塞
  The freeways were clogged with traffic.
  快车道被来往的车辆阻塞了。
  In fact your memory is clogged with useless things.
  事实上，你的记忆中塞满了无用的东西。

11. bring home the bacon： 成功，谋生
  He is the only one that brings home the bacon.
  他是惟一养家糊口的人。

12. as a result of： 作为结果
  He died as a direct result of the accident.
  他的死是那次事故的直接结果。
  As a result of what I saw I decided to leave him.
  鉴于我所看到的事实，我决定离开他。

13. wipe out： 扫除，清除掉，使消失
  wipe out an unpleasant memory：抹掉令人不快的回忆
  wipe out the enemy's major military targets：彻底摧毁敌人的主要军事目标
  wipe out the coffee pot：把咖啡壶里面洗干净

14. in honour of： 为纪念，为庆祝
  We had a banquet in honour of the delegation.
  我们设宴招待代表团。
  They put up a monument in honour of those killed in battle.
  他们为纪念阵亡将士建造了一座纪念碑。

15. on resuming office： 恢复工作时
  resume his place in society：恢复他原先的社会地位
  resume control over the area：重新控制该地区

16. look back on： 回顾
  I like to look back on those unforgettable years in the army.
  我喜欢回忆在部队的那些难忘的岁月。
  She still shudders when she looks back on the past.
  想起过去，她仍然不寒而栗。
