1. compatibility computer： 处理男女般配的计算机
  意思是用计算机将男女双方的条件对比，使之般配。

2. blurt out： 脱口而出
  I blurted out the secret.
  我不慎说出了秘密。

3. look through： 翻阅，看一遍
  Before the meeting I looked through the reports.
  开会前，我把报告看了一遍。
  She spent the whole night looking through the students' homework.
  她花了整个晚上看学生的作业。

4. an attempt at： 在……上的一次尝试
  My attempt at a Christmas cake had to be thrown away.
  我试着做的圣诞蛋糕只能扔掉了。
  He made an attempt at escaping in vain.
  他企图逃跑，但那是白费力气。

5. go to such lengths to do something： 如此费力气地做某事
  They went to great lengths to ensure a quiet surrounding in the dormitory.
  他们费了很大的精力来确保宿舍周围安静。
  He went to the lengths of perjuring for her sake.
  他为了她甚至作了伪证。

6. put impossible demands on the opposite side： 对另一方提出不可能实现的要求
  meet one's demand：满足某人的要求
  This work makes many demands on my time.
  这工作要花费我很长时间。
  If we grow less, we'll not be able to meet the demand of the market.
  我们如果种少了，就不能满足市场的需要。

7. go to the trouble of doing something： 不怕麻烦地做某事
  I'd rather not go to the trouble of bringing in my luggage.
  我宁愿不把行李带进来，那太麻烦了。
  Your company should go to the trouble of doing whatever is necessary to make the bikes acceptable to US Customs.
  你们公司应该想方设法地进行一切必要工作，让自行车合乎美国海关的要求。

8. put the blame on somebody： 把过失推到某人身上
  They put the blame on that poor boy.
  他们把责任都推到那个可怜的小男孩身上。
  You should lay the blame on the right shoulder.
  你应当指责该受指责的人。
