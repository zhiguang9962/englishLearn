1. be valued at： 被估价为……
  The house is valued at ￡95000.
  这房子被估价为95000英镑。
  I valued the ring at $80.
  我估计这枚戒指值80美元。

2. push one's way through the crowd： 从人群中挤过去
  He pushed himself to the front of the crowd.
  他挤到人群的前边。
  I pushed my way past and went into the room.
  我挤了过去，走进房间。

3. break into the safe： 撬开保险柜
  A house was broken into between midnight and 4 am.
  在午夜到凌晨4点这段时间，有人闯入了一所房子。
  They broke into the palaces and snatched everything of value.
  他们闯进皇宫，抢走了所有值钱的东西。

4. put ... on the right track： 将……引入正轨
  It was Mendel who put us on the right track by his assumption that there are factors inside organisms which specify the characters we observe.
  是孟德尔将我们引入正轨，他认为有机体内部的元素详细说明了我们所观察到的事物特征。
  Furthermore, they thought they were on the right track, and went ahead with the flight.
  而且，他们认为自己处于正确的航道，便继续向前飞。

5. wet to the skin： 湿透了
 
6. lie face upwards： 仰面躺着
  I lay sprawled on my back.
  我伸开四肢仰面躺着。
  She lay on her side with her face opposite me.
  她侧躺着，脸对着我。

7. no use doing something： 做某事没有用
  There is no use reading novels all day long.
  整天看小说是没用的。
  When you meet with difficulties in your work, it is no use losing heart.
  工作遇到困难的时候，灰心是没有用的。

8. sooner or later： 迟早
  Sooner or later this kid will plunge to death.
  这个孩子迟早要摔死。

9. agree to（to作介词）： 同意，允诺（某一建议、安排等）
  He agreed to my proposal.
  他同意了我的建议。
  Under no circumstances can we agree to such a principle.
  在任何情况下我们也不会同意这样的原则。

10. be sick and tired of： 对……感到厌烦
  I am sick and tired of doing anything.
  我对做所有的事情都感到了厌烦。
  She is sick and tired of all the blonde jokes.
  她对所有关于金发碧眼女人的笑话感到厌烦。

11. no point in running away： 没有任何理由逃走
  There is no point in crying over spilt milk.
  为打翻的牛奶而哭是没有用的。（喻：覆水难收。）
  What's the point of spending such a lot of money on clothes?
  花这么多钱在穿着上有什么用呢？

12. break down： 悲痛欲绝（本课中的意思）；（机器等）坏了；（谈话）中断；压倒，破除
  I broke down and sobbed aloud.
  我受不了，大声哭了起来。
  My mobile phone frequently broke down.
  我的手机经常坏。
  She broke down in the middle of her speech.
  她讲了半截停了下来。
  He put out a call for breaking down fetishes and superstitions and emancipating the mind.
  他发出破除迷信，解放思想的号召。

13. the only way out： 惟一的解决办法
  What could have happened to this girl to make her feel she had no way out?
  这个女孩到底出了什么事，为何会觉得走投无路？
  Give the wrong-doer a way out.
  网开一面。

14. in case： 万一
  Take a hat with you in case the sun is very hot.
  倘若阳光很强，你就把帽子戴上。
  Take along an umbrella, just in case.
  随身带一把伞，以防万一。
