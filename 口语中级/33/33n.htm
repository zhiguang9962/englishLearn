1. set back： 花费（本课中的意思）；阻碍，使受挫折
  The legal costs of the case set me back $10000.
  这场官司花了我1万美元。
  It's impossible to set back the clock of history.
  要使历史车轮倒转是不可能的。

2. on the lookout for a soft touch： 伺机等待唾手可得的东西
  We are on the lookout for a quality manufacturer.
  我们在寻找优秀的厂商。
  We have since alerted our field personnel to be on the lookout for "cell phone guns" at U.S. ports of entry.
  从那时起我们就要求海关人员严防这种“手机手枪”在美国登陆。

3. hardened "professionals"： 死不悔改的“惯犯”
  small-time criminals：轻罪犯

4. set out with particular targets in mind： 着手于脑中特定的目标
  To set out in business is no easy job.
  经商伊始，事业不易。
  That winter she set out to write a novel.
  那年冬天，她着手写一部小说。

5. in short： 简而言之
  In short, I didn't know which one to pick.
  总之，我不知道要选哪一个。
  It is called deoxyribonucleic acid, or DNA for short.
  它被称作脱氧核糖核酸，或简称DNA。

6. carry out： 完成，实现，执行
  We also carried out other experiments.
  我们还进行了其他实验。

7. contribute ... to： 把……贡献给、捐赠给
  We all expressed our desire to contribute our part to the prosperity of our motherland.
  我们都表示要为祖国的繁荣贡献出一份力量。
  He contributed generously to refugee relief.
  他为救济难民慷慨捐赠。

8. in short supply： 供应不足
  Anodyne was in extremely short supply.
  止痛药曾极度缺乏。

9. in addition to： 除……之外
  In addition to the apples you asked for, I bought you some bananas.
  除了你要的苹果外，我还给你买了些香蕉。

10. take turns (in) doing something： 轮流做某事
  We decided to take turns in looking after the baby.
  我们决定轮流照顾那婴儿。
