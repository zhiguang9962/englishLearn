1. in the middle of the night： 在半夜里
  Please stand in the middle of the room.
  请站在房子中间。
  He is always in the middle of the road.
  他总是保持中立。

2. the sort that burns beautifully： （= the kind of house that burns easily）
  特别容易着火的那种（房子）
  在口语里beautifully 作“极好地”解。
  The car is running beautifully.
  这辆汽车性能好极了。

3. on the top floor： 在顶楼
  ground floor：一层（美语是first floor）

4. jump out of bed： 跳下床
  He jumped to his feet and ran off.
  他跳起来跑掉了。
  I jumped off the train and ran towards him.
  我跳下火车，朝他跑了过去。

5. step out into the passage： 从屋里出来走到走廊上
  I'm just going to step out for a minute or two.
  我只出去一两分钟。
  She stepped into the room and locked the door.
  她走进屋把门锁上。
  Please step this way.
  请这边走。

6. be full of thick smoke： 浓烟密布
  thick hair：浓密的头发
  a thick forest：密林
  a thick speech：口齿不清的讲话

7. be half-awake： 半梦半醒的
  half-cooked adj. 半生不熟的
  half-blooded adj. 同父异母的（或同母异父的）
  half-hearted adj. 缺乏热情的，半心半意的

8. in the opposite direction： 以相反的方向
  He went off in one direction and she in another.
  他朝一个方向走，她则朝另一个方向走。
  When Inspector Li arrived, the crowd scattered in all directions.
  李警官一到，人群就向四面八方散开了。

9. One of my feet caught in something soft.
  我的一只脚让一个软软的东西绊住了。
  A nail caught her skirts.
  钉子钩住了她的裙子。
  The kite caught in a tree.
  风筝挂在树上了。

10. feel like something： 摸上去如同某物，感觉像是……的样子
  It feels like rain.
  像是要下雨了。
  She felt like a fool.
  她觉得自己像个傻子似的。

11. protect ... from ...： 保护……不受到……
  Tom raised his arm to protect himself from the blow.
  汤姆举起手臂护住自己，以免被这一拳打中。
  The doctor told me to protect my eyes from the sun.
  医生叫我保护眼睛，不要让太阳照射。

12. give way： 垮掉，倒塌（本课中的意思）；退让，妥协
  The ice gave way and they fell into the water.
  冰一塌陷，他们就掉进水里了。
  At last I had to give way to him.
  最后，我只得向他妥协。

13. get through： 通过（本课中的意思）；结束；看完
  The bill has got through.
  这项法案已经通过。
  I'll be with you as soon as I get through with this work.
  这项工作一搞完我就来。
  Have you got through the book?
  你看完这本书了吗？

14. give a thin cry： 发出微弱的哭声
  thin hair：稀疏的头发
  a thin time：不好受的日子（因为发生令人不快的事情）
  a thin story：乏味的故事；不能自圆其说的借口

15. in my surprise： 在惊讶中……
  To my great surprise, the plan succeeded.
  我十分惊奇的是那计划竟然成功了。
  He looked up in surprise when I shouted.
  我叫喊时，他吃惊地抬头看。
  The gift came as a complete surprise to me.
  这件礼物完全出乎我的意料。

16. She saw me and came running madly.
  她看见了我，并发疯一般地跑了过来。
  There a warm wind came blowing over the boundless sea.
  从无边的海上吹来一阵暖风。
  Torrents of water came rushing down from the mountains.
  山洪奔腾而来。

17. have some difficulty in doing something： 做某事有点费劲
  At first, he had great difficulty in persuading her to go to Shanghai.
  一开始，他很难说服她去上海。
  Do you have any difficulty with English?
  你英语上有困难吗？

18. two stairs at a time： 一步跨两级台阶

19. run up： 向上跑（本课中的意思）；积累；抬高
  He ran up a bill for over $300.
  他总共欠账300多美元。
  He tried to run up the price of the company's stock.
  他设法抬高公司股票的价格。

20. throw open the door： 用力推开房门
  The swimming pool will be thrown open to the public next week.
  这游泳池下周将对外开放。
