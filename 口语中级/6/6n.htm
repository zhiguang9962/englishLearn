1. be busy doing something (=be busy with something)： 忙于做某事
  He's busy writing letters.
  他正忙着写信呢。
  She's busy with her homework.
  她正忙着做家庭作业。

2. set schedule： 固定时间
  ahead of schedule：比计划提前
  on schedule：准时
  behind schedule：比计划晚

3. get along with somebody： 和某人相处
  Can you get along with him?
  你和他处得来吗？
  We have been getting along well with each other.
  我们一直相处得很好。

4. end up： 以……告终，最后……
  At first he refused to accept any responsibility but he ended up apologizing.
  最初他拒不承认有任何责任，到头来还是道了歉。
  If you go on doing that kind of thing, you'll end up in prison.
  你如果继续这样干，一定会落个进监狱的下场。

5. pay one's bill： 付账单
  Thanks for paying my bill.
  谢谢为我付账。
  How much do you pay for rent?
  你交多少房租？
  I'll pay you in a couple of days.
  我一两天就把钱付给你。

6. leave somebody out of something： 让某人从某事中解脱出来。
  This left me without a ray of hope.
  这使我失去了一切希望。
  All the while I was left in the dark about the case.
  这事我一直都被蒙在鼓里。

7. leave out： 漏掉，删掉（本课中的feel left out引申义为“感觉被忽略”）
  The alterations are small. Nothing valuable is left out.
  改动不大，没有删掉什么有价值的东西。
  You've made a mistake ― you've left out the letter f.
  你出了一个错，把字母f漏掉了。

8. a replacement for somebody： 替代某人
  the replacement of conventional weapons by nuclear weapons：用核武器取代常规武器
  Jane is ill, and we have to find a replacement for her.
  简病了，我们必须找个代她工作的人。

9. have something to do with： 和……有关系
  She advised me to have nothing to do with him.
  她劝我不要和他有什么联系。
  You'd better not have too much to do with those people.
  你最好不要和那些人有太多来往。

10. on an equal footing： 平等
  The children are under my feet all day.
  孩子们整天烦我。
  After her husband's death it took her three years to get back on her feet.
  她丈夫死后三年，她才振作起来。

11. bring up： 抚养，培养（本课中的意思）；提出；呕吐
  I was brought up in the city.
  我是在城市里长大的。
  Why bring that up again?
  又提那干吗？
  I felt sick and brought up what I had eaten in the mooring.
  我觉得恶心，把早晨吃的东西都吐出来了。

12. be mad at： 对……愤怒
  I was mad at him for coming home late.
  我为他回家迟了而发火。

13. make ends meet： 收支相抵
  Being out of work and having two young children, she found it impossible to make ends meet.
  她失了业，还要养活两个孩子，无法维持起码的生活。

14. be vivid in one's mind： 对……记忆犹新
  It is still vivid in my memory.
  这事我还记得很清楚。

15. see my parents' true colors： 看到我父母的真实面目
  At last she showed her true colors.
  最后，她露出了她的本来面目。
  His story has some color of truth.
  他的一番叙述听来似乎是真的。

16. hide something from somebody： 对某人隐瞒某事
  Are you trying to hide something from me?
  你是不是有什么事想瞒着我？

17. be apt to do something： 倾向于做某事
  She is apt to get excited over trifles.
  她容易为小事而激动。
  My pen is rather apt to leak.
  我的钢笔爱漏墨水。

18. level with somebody： 对某人说实话
  They tried to advise the suspect to level with the authorities.
  他们努力劝服嫌疑犯对当局坦白。

19. Rolling Stones： 滚石乐队
