1. die of a heart attack： 死于心脏病
  Her grandfather died of starvation before liberation.
  解放前她的祖父饿死了。
  He died from a wound.
  他受伤致死。

2. do ... about： 对……采取某种行动
  I really don't know what to do about it.
  我真不知道该把它怎么办。
  You ought to do something about your cold.
  你该治治你的感冒。

3. in addition： 此外
  In addition there are five other applicants.
  此外还有五个申请人。
  In addition to genes, intelligence also depends on an adequate diet, a good education and a decent home environment.
  除了遗传基因外，智力的高低还取决于充足的营养、优等的教育和良好的家庭环境。

4. with the provision that ...： 以……的条件
  I accepted the contract with the provision that it would be revised after six months.
  我同意签订这份合同，其中规定半年后得加以修订。
  Both sides have to act according to the provisions of the agreement.
  双方都应按照协议条款办事。

5. as long as： 只要，如果
  Stay as long as you like.
  你愿停留多久都可以。
  As long as it doesn't rain we can have a sports meeting.
  只要不下雨，我们就能开运动会。

6. act up： 犯病，运作不正常
  My sprained ankle has been acting up badly all week.
  我扭伤的脚踝整整痛了一个星期。
  The machine is acting up again.
  这台机器又出毛病了。

7. take care of： 照顾（本课中的意思）；负责，处理
  She is taking care of her little sister while her parents are away.
  她父母不在的时候，由她来照看小妹妹。
  He takes care of marketing and publicity.
  他负责产品的销售和推广。

8. make efforts to do something： 努力做某事
  I will make every effort to be a good wife.
  我将尽一切努力做一名好妻子。
  You must put more effort into your work.
  你必须更加努力工作。

9. result in： 结果，导致
  These measures resulted in tremendous changes in many of our products.
  由于采取了这些措施，我们的许多产品发生了巨大变化。
  The accident resulted in three deaths.
  这次事故造成三人死亡。
  Our efforts resulted in failure.
  我们的努力以失败告终。

10. blame ... on ...： 把……归咎于……
  They blamed the crisis on their poor planning.
  他们把危机归咎于计划不周。
  Don't blame it on him, but on me.
  别怪他，该怪我。

11. put one's mind at rest： 放宽心，安心
  You won't succeed with your work if you don't put your mind on it.
  你要是不用心，这工作你是干不好的。
  She was at rest in a country churchyard.
  她安息在乡间的教堂墓地里。

12. the poverty-stricken areas： 贫困地区

13. the majority of： 大部分
  The majority of people seem to prefer TV to radio.
  大部分人似乎喜欢看电视而不喜欢听收音机。
  The majority were in favour of the proposal.
  多数人赞成这个建议。
