1. Oh, come on ... it's something.
  噢，得了，肯定有什么事情。
  Come on. She is waiting.
  赶快。她在等着呢。
  Come on, Tom, come on. Don't be so shy.
  来吧，汤姆，来吧。别不好意思。

2. take ... for a walk： 带……散步
  Every morning she takes the dog for a walk.
  每天早晨她都带着狗去散步。
  He had a pleasant walk across the fields.
  他愉快地走过田野。

3. meet ... at the station： 到车站接……
  He went to the station to meet her.
  他到车站去接她了。
  She said she was to meet the six o'clock train.
  她说她要去接六点的火车。

4. pay somebody a visit： 拜访某人
  It's so good of you to pay me a visit.
  你能来看我真是太好了。
  They paid a visit to Canada in September.
  他们九月访问了加拿大。

5. be denied a chance to ...： 被剥夺……的机会
  He was very sad to be denied this opportunity.
  没得到这个机会，他非常难过。
  Many people were thus denied the right to vote.
  就这样很多人被剥夺了选举权。

6. a working knowledge of English： 能应用英语

7. be inferior to： 次于，劣于
  A captain is inferior to a major.
  上尉的级别低于少校。
  These goods are inferior to the sample.
  这些货物质地不如样品。

8. decide against： 决定不……
  At the meeting we decided against carrying out the plan.
  在会上我们决定不执行这项计划。
  I decided to give up my job.
  我决定放弃工作。

9. all the more： 更加
  He smoked all the more for his personal and family problems.
  由于他的个人和家庭问题，他抽烟抽得更凶了。
  We'll have to work all the harder with two members of staff away ill.
  有两名职员因病缺勤，我们只好加把劲儿。

10. accuse somebody of ...： 谴责，控告某人……
  I accused him of having broken his word.
  我指责他没有遵守诺言。
  Who accused you of such a thing?
  谁因为这样的事谴责你？

11. sex discrimination： 性别歧视
  social discrimination：社会歧视，社会不平等待遇
  race discrimination：种族歧视

12. be on one's own： 靠自己的力量，独立地
  I got the job on my own.
  我自己找到了这份工作。
  He is now out of college and on his own.
  他现在离开了大学并且独立生活。

13. sympathize with： 同情，同感，同意
  I sympathize with you because I've had a similar unhappy experience myself.
  我同情你，因为我自己也有过类似的不幸遭遇。
  We have long sympathized with the aims of the Green Party.
  我们长期以来一直支持绿党保护生态环境的目标。

14. at a disadvantage： 处于不利的地位
  His inability to speak English puts him at a disadvantage when he attends international conferences.
  他不会说英语，这不利于他参加国际会议。
  She is at a disadvantage because she is much older than the other candidate.
  她处于不利地位，因为她比另一位候选人年龄大多了。

15. take in： 吸收（本课中的意思）；欺骗，使上当；订阅（报刊）；领会
  We plan to take in more new Party members this year.
  我们计划今年吸收更多的新党员。
  She was completely taken in by his story.
  她完全被他的谎言欺骗了。
  My mother took in a daily paper.
  妈妈订了一份日报。
  I listened to the lecture, but did not take in much.
  我听了讲座，但没听懂多少。

16. a carton of high-grade cigarettes： 一条高级烟

17. maternity leave： 产假

18. break into tears at something： 为某事哭
  At this point even the interpreter broke into tears.
  这时，连译员都哭了。
  We broke into applause when he finished his report.
  他做完报告后，我们都鼓起掌来。
 
19. quarrel with somebody over something： 为某事和某人吵架
  Don't quarrel with him over such a trifling matter.
  别和他为这样的小事争吵了。

20. a barrage of protests and condemnation： 一连串的抗议和谴责
  a barrage of questions：连珠炮似的问题
  a barrage of criticism：接二连三的一大堆批评

21. tear ... to pieces： 把……撕成碎片
  He was so angry that he tore the letter into tiny pieces.
  他气得把信撕得粉碎。

22. come to one's rescue： 帮某人一把
  We came to his rescue and pulled him out of the river.
  我们来救他，把他从河里拉了出来。
  A wealthy sponsor came to our rescue with a generous donation.
  一位富有的赞助人以慷慨捐赠解救了我们。

23. not by a long shot： 完全不，绝不（表示不相信时的用语）
  We're not beaten yet, not by a long shot.
  我们还没有败，远远没败。
