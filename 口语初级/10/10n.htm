1. go shopping： 去买东西
  She used to have a window shopping without buying anything.
  她过去常常游览商店橱窗，什么东西都不买。
  I will do my shopping this Sunday.
  这星期日我要去购物。

2. tell somebody about something： 告诉某人关于……的事情
  Tell me about your new job.
  告诉我有关你新工作的情况。
  He has been able to tell us more about fossil remains than any man alive.
  他现在比任何人都能更多地向我们讲述有关化石的情况。

3. How much does it cost?
  多少钱？
  That suit costs me over ￡6.
  那件上衣花掉我6英镑。
  That furniture really costs.
  那家具很贵。

4. come back from work： 下班回来
  Tom comes back from school at four every afternoon.
  汤姆每天下午四点放学回家。
  He came home very late from his office yesterday.
  他昨天很晚才下班回家。

5. continue to do something： 继续做某事
  continue to do something 与 continue doing something 的区别在于前者是指
  “继续不断地做某事”，后者是说“持续做同一件事情”。
  We should continue to improve ourselves in English.
  我们应该不断提高自己的英语水平。
  Don't continue working more than five hours.
  不要持续工作超过5个小时。

6. at last： 终于
  At last, I accepted his suggestion.
  最后，我接受了他的建议。
  At long last the winter was over.
  冬季总算过去了。

7. a little better： （比原来）好了一些
  a little 除了限定不可数名词外，还可用来修饰比较级，表示“稍微（比……）”的意思，
  与其相对的是 much + 比较级。
  He's a little better this morning.
  他今天早晨稍微好了一些。
  That's much better.
  那样好多了。

8. catch a number 7 bus： 乘7 路汽车
  Did you catch a bus to town?
  你赶上去城里的汽车了吗？
  I was caught in traffic for an hour.
  我赶上交通堵塞，耽误了一小时。

9. take somebody to some place： 带某人去某地
  Jane took her children to the park last Sunday.
  简上星期天带孩子去了公园。
  She takes her son to school by car.
  她用汽车送儿子上学。

10. wait for half an hour at the bus stop： 在汽车站等了半个小时
  I am waiting for the guests to arrive.
  我正在等待客人的到来。
  Let's wait up for the stragglers.
  让我们等等掉队的人。

11. leave from the centre of town： 远离市中心
  I am leaving (from) America.
  我要告别美国了。
  Mary loves to be the centre of interest.
  玛丽喜欢成为人们注意的中心。

12. walk two miles： 步行两英里
  I walk the financial district of a city every day.
  我每天都要穿行过城内的金融区。
  I used to walk 3 miles one hour a day.
  我过去常常每天一小时步行三英里。
