1. be bad for： 对……不好
  Reading in bed is bad for your eyes.
  躺在床上看书对眼睛不好。
  Smoking is bad for your health.
  抽烟有害健康。

2. bring somebody something (bring something to / for somebody)： 带给某人某物
  Bring me the book.
  把那本书带给我。
  He brought a beautiful present for me.
  他给我带来一件漂亮的礼物。

3. visit somebody / some place： 拜访某人 / 参观某地
  I visited my friends in town.
  我去看望了城里的朋友。
  I am going to visit the History Museum this Sunday.
  我打算这周日去参观历史博物馆。

4. let somebody do something： 让某人做某事
  let 和 make 一样，其动词不定式不带 to。
  Just let her try!
  就让她试试吧！
  Please let me go!
  请让我走吧！

5. say one's prayers： 做祈祷
  She says her prayers before she gets into bed.
  她上床睡觉前做祈祷。
  One evening a week, the family would join together in prayer.
  这家人每星期都要有一个晚上聚在一起做祈祷。

6. give somebody something for： 为了……给某人某物
  We gave her flowers for her birthday.
  我们送她一些花以庆祝她的生日。
  I will give ten dollars for the desk.
  我愿付十美元买这张书桌。

7. hear somebody doing something： 听见某人正在做某事
  大多感官动词，如 see，feel，hear 等，后面的宾语常用现在分词结构，表示“看见 / 感觉 /
  听到某人正在做某事”。
  I heard her singing that song.
  我听见她在唱那首歌。
  I saw him running across the road when I left the store.
  离开商店的时候我看到他正跑过马路。

8. "I know," answered the clever boy with a smile.
  “我知道，”小男孩面带微笑地回答。（倒装句）
  He jumped with joy when he heard the good news.
  听到这个好消息时他高兴得跳起来。
  She asked with a doubtful expression.
  她带着疑惑的表情问道。

9. in the next room： 在隔壁房间
  He sat next to her.
  他坐在她的旁边。
  They live next door to us.
  他们住在我们隔壁。

10. the rest of the Festival： 剩下的节日活动
  a film festival：电影节
  the Lantern Festival：元宵节
  the Spring Festival：春节
  the Moon Festival：中秋节

11. go to a pub for a final drink： 到一个酒馆最后喝一杯
  Can I have a drink?
  我能来杯饮料吗？
  He was thirsty and took a long drink from the fountain.
  他很渴，喝了一大口泉水。

12. leave a message for somebody： 给某人留条
  Would you leave a message for him?
  你要给他留条吗？
  He left a note for you.
  他给你留了一张便条。

13. go round to some place / somebody： 参观某处 / 拜访某人
  I am going round to one of my friends later.
  我打算稍后到我一个朋友那儿看看。
  I went round to his new house yesterday.
  我昨天去了他的新家。

14. feel / be pleased with oneself： 对自己（做的事情）颇为得意
  He won praises from others and felt pleased with himself.
  他赢得了其他人的赞扬，并洋洋得意。
  Are you satisfied with yourself?
  你对自己满意吗？

15. How did it go, then?
  那么进行得如何？
  How are things going?
  情况如何？
  Everything goes well.
  一切都好。

16. take a photograph / photo / picture： 照相
  I took a lot of photographs on my vacation.
  度假的时候我拍了很多照片。
  He took a close-up picture for her.
  他给她拍了个特写镜头。

17. win the competition： 赢得比赛
  Who won the race?
  谁在赛跑中获胜了？
  He won a prize in a competition of chess.
  他在国际象棋比赛中得了奖。

18. had better do something： 最好做某事
  We'd better not invite him.
  我们最好不要邀请他。
  You'd better stay at hospital for another few days.
  你最好在医院再住几天。

19. too ... to ...： 太……而不能……
  You are too young to join the army.
  你太小了，不能参军。
  He is too careless to notice that.
  他太粗心了，没有注意到那一点。
