1. be three miles from ...： 距……三英里远
  The new theatre is only a few hundred metres from our school.
  新剧院离我们学校只有几百米远。
  My father's office is five kilometres from our house.
  我父亲的事务所离我们家有5公里远。

2. stand in a clearing in the woods： 位于树林中的一块空地
  The building stands at the corner.
  那座大楼位于拐角处。
  The house stands at the top of the hill.
  这座房子坐落在小山顶上。

3. hold about twenty-five boys and girls： 容纳大概25名男女学生
  The room can hold twenty people.
  这屋子可容下20个人。
  This bottle holds one liter.
  这个瓶子装一升。

4. attend the school： 上学
  Please attend the meeting on time.
  请准时出席会议。
  I attended a lecture yesterday evening; it was boring.
  我昨天晚上听了一堂讲座，很无聊。

5. with more or less regularity： 多少有些规律
  We hope our explanation will prove more or less helpful.
  希望我们的说明或多或少有些帮助。
  I've more or less finished reading the book.
  我差不多已经把这本书看完了。

6. once or twice a week： 一星期一两次
  I go to see a film once a week.
  我每周看一场电影。
  He comes to visit me once or twice a month.
  他一个月来看我一两次。

7. in the cool of the morning： 在凉爽的早晨
  in a cool autumn evening：在一个清新的秋夜

8. in the dusk： 黄昏时
  It is difficult to see clearly at dusk.
  在黄昏时很难看清东西。
  He worked from dawn to dusk
  他从早干到晚。
  I often go out for a walk in the dusk of the evening.
  我常常在黄昏时分出去散步。

9. sit in the shade of the trees： 坐在树荫下
  Keep the medicine in the shade.
  把药放在阴凉处保存。
  The shades of night are falling fast.
  夜幕很快降临。

10. at noon： 中午
  At noon, the sun is high in the sky.
  正午，烈日当空。
  He is in the noon of life.
  他正当壮年时期。

11. look back upon / on： 回顾
  I always remember Mary when I look back on my childhood.
  我总是在回顾童年的时候想起玛丽。
  I hate looking back on the past.
  我讨厌回顾过去。

12. with the most satisfaction： 怀着极大的满足
  He looked at his work with a smile of satisfaction.
  他看着他的工作，露出满意的微笑。

13. a strapping girl of fifteen： 一个15岁的高个儿女孩
  It is a dress of silk.
  它是一件丝制的衣服。
  She is a woman of great charm.
  她是一个妩媚的女人。

14. in the customary sunbonnet and calico dress： 戴着普通的太阳帽，穿着印花布的连衣裙
  The old woman dressed in rags.
  这位老妇人衣衫褴褛。
  The man in the top hat first made a speech.
  戴着大礼帽的那人首先发表了演说。

15. rouse her scorn： 激起她的蔑视
  I was roused to anger by the insult.
  我由于受到侮辱而被激怒。
  His words roused me to interest in the film.
  他的话激起了我对那部电影的兴趣。

16. report me to： 向……告我的状
  He reported me to the head.
  他向领导告发我。
 
17. chew tobacco： 嚼烟草
  Chew your food well before you swallow it.
  食物在吞咽前要仔细咀嚼。

18. fairly well： 相当好
  He paints fairly well.
  他画得相当好。
  The food is fairly delicious.
  这种食品挺好吃的。

19. win over： 争取，说服
  She's against the idea, but I'm sure I can win her over.
  她反对这个意见，但我准能说服她改变主意。

20. I remained a poor thing.
  我仍是一个可怜的小东西。
  Her death still remains a mystery.
  她的死仍然是个谜。
  What he said remained in doubt.
  他所说的仍然值得怀疑。

21. long to： 渴望
  I am longing to go to watch the football match.
  我极想去观看足球赛。
  He longed for a camera.
  他特想要架照相机。

22. rise： vi. （在社会上、职业上等）升至较高的级别
  In the early 1960's his family rose in New York society.
  他的家族于二十世纪六十年代初期在纽约社会兴起。
  He rose from an ordinary clerk to become a project manager.
  他由普通职员升为项目经理。

23. have but little charity： 几乎没有宽容
  Their hopes lasted but a moment.
  他们的希望仅仅持续了片刻。
  The problem is anything but easy.
  这个问题可绝对不容易。

24. one another： 彼此，相互
  The students help one another.
  学生彼此帮助。
  The people followed one another into the room.
  人们一个接一个地进了房间。

25. on a camping holiday： 野营度假
  When I was on holiday, I visited my parents.
  我在度假的时候去看望了父母。
  I have to prepare the camping equipment for my trip to Greece.
  我得为我的希腊之行准备露营装备。

26. bring something with somebody： 某人身带某物
  Do you bring enough money with you?
  你身上带够钱了吗？
  He brought a new book with him.
  他带来一本新书。

27. only a little： 一点点
  与 quite a little 意思相反，only a little 比 a little 更强调量少，它们都修饰
  不可数名词。quite a few，a few，only a few 用于修饰可数名词。
  There is only a little time left.
  只有很短的时间了。

28. by the way： 顺便说一下。
  By the way, what happened to that boy?
  顺便问一句，那男孩后来怎么样了？
