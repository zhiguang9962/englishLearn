1. Christmas cards： 圣诞贺卡
  on Christmas Eve：在圣诞节前夕
  Father Christmas：圣诞老人
  at Christmas (time)：在圣诞节
  Merry Christmas!
  圣诞快乐！

2. send to： 寄给，送给
  David bought a large tin of orange paint and had it sent to John for his new kitchen.
  戴维买了一大罐橙色油漆送给约翰粉刷他的新厨房。
  Mary sent some knitting wool to her daughter for her birthday.
  玛丽送给女儿一些羊毛针织品作为她的生日礼物。

3. Here are the Christmas cards for our friends.
  这些是送给我们朋友的圣诞贺卡。（倒装句）
  Here is the change for you.
  这是找给你的零钱。
  Here's a health to you!
  （敬酒时用语）祝你健康！

4. while： conj. 当……的时候
  While in Paris he studied painting.
  在巴黎期间，他学习了绘画。
  I met her while I was having my lunch at school.
  在学校吃午饭的时候我遇见了她。

5. admire： v. 赞美，欣赏
  Aren't you going to admire my new hat?
  你难道不想夸夸我的新帽子吗？
  I admire her for her bravery.
  我钦佩她的勇气。

6. invite somebody to (do) something： 邀请某人做某事
  I invited him to sing a song.
  我邀请他唱首歌。
  I am going to invite some friends to dinner this Saturday.
  这周六我打算请一些朋友来吃饭。

7. New Year resolutions： 年初所下的决心
  He is a man of great resolution.
  他是个极其果断的人。
  She's always making good resolutions.
  她决心总是下得很好。

8. have a good answer for somebody： 很好地回击某人
  Our only possible answer was to sue.
  我们唯一可能的反击就是上诉。
  I have a complete answer to this accusation.
  我能证明这种指控纯系无中生有。
 
9. five-and-ten-cent store：
  出售廉价小商品的杂货店（原先商品的价格均为五分、一角，故有此名）

10. break： v. 打破
  He never breaks faith with his clients.
  他对顾客从来没有失信过。
  He said he would come back, but he broke his promise.
  他说他会回来，可他食言了。

11. say something to somebody： 对某人说某事
  I want to say a few words to you.
  我想和你说几句话。
  You should learn to say "Thank you" to others.
  你应该学会对别人说“谢谢”。
