1. insurance office (= insurance company)： 保险公司
  endowment insurance：养老保险
  labour insurance：劳动保险
  insurance for medical care：医疗保险
  unemployment insurance：失业保险

2. have something done： 让（某人）做某事
  I had my hair cut yesterday.
  我昨天（让人）理发了。
  We had the machine repaired.
  我们请人把机器修好了。
 
3. die at the age of thirty： 于三十岁去世
  die of tuberculosis：死于肺结核
  die from a wound：受伤不治而亡
  die for one's country：为国捐躯
  die by one's own hand：自杀

4. as： conj. 因为；当……时
  I went to bed early yesterday, as I was exhausted.
  我昨天睡得早，因为我精疲力尽了。
  She was willing to do anything for him as she loved him.
  她愿意为他做任何事情，因为她爱他。
  I slipped on the ice as I ran to the school.
  我跑向学校时在冰上滑了一跤。
  I dropped my glasses as I stood up.
  我站起来时，把我的眼镜摔了。

5. No office will insure you if you speak like that.
  如果你像那样说话，没有哪家公司会给你担保。（完全否定）
  No water is left.
  一点水也没剩下。
  No one can do it.
  没有人能做此事。

6. Use your imagination a little.
  稍微发挥一下你的想像力。
  It is completely beyond my imagination.
  事情完全出乎我的意料。
  What you said was not true; it was just your imagination.
  你所说的不是真的，那只是你的想像。

7. be shown into： 被带进
  They were shown into the parlor.
  他们被带进客厅。
  Please show this lady out of the building.
  请把这位太太送出去。

8. a fall off her bicycle： 从她的自行车上摔下来
  He suffered a fall from his horse.
  他从马背上跌了下来。
  There has been a fall in the price of food.
  食品的价格有所下跌。

9. deliver： v. 送，运送
  Some new books have been delivered to the school.
  一些新书已被送到学校。
  Does the store deliver free of charge?
  这家商店免费送货吗？

10. it depends ...： 依……而定
  It all depends (on) how you deal with it.
  那要看你如何处理这事而定。
  A grade will depend on the results of the final exam.
  分级将由期终考试的结果而定。
