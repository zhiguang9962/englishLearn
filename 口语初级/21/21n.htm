1. lean back： 向后仰靠
  She is leaning against the railing.
  她正倚在栏杆上。
  I don't want to lean on you for help.
  我不想依赖你的帮助。

2. Would you mind ...?
  你是否在意……？
  Would you mind my coming with you?
  你是否介意我和你一起走？
  Would you mind opening the window?
  请你打开一下窗户好不好？

3. take your feet off the table： 把你的脚从桌子上拿下来
  I took the vase off the table as he said he didn't like it.
  我把花瓶从桌子上拿走了，因为他说他不喜欢。
  I felt ill this morning and took a day off.
  我今天早晨不舒服，请了一天假。
  Take off your clothes; they're very wet.
  脱掉你的衣服，衣服湿透了。

4. Not at all.
  一点儿也不。
  此句话实际上是 not ... at all 的省略写法，在口语中常用到。

5. avoid doing something： 避免做某事
  You should avoid making the same mistake.
  你应该避免犯同样的错误。
  I didn't go there any more to avoid meeting him.
  我没再去那儿，避免碰见他。

6. How much do you think it's worth?
  你觉得它值多少钱？
  The old television is only worth $30.
  那台旧电视就值30美元。
  Here's my suggestion, for what it's worth.
  这是我的提议，尽管可能用处不大，你姑且听听。

7. look forward to： 盼望
  I am looking forward to meeting you again.
  我期待着与你的再次见面。
  She is looking forward to a trip to Beijing.
  她盼望着北京之旅。

8. a relaxing evening： 一个令人放松的晚上
  注意 relax，relaxed，relaxing 三个词的词性变化。relax 既可作为及物动词，也可作为
  不及物动词。relaxed 和 relaxing 都是形容词，前者是“放松的”，常用于说人的精神状态；
  后者是“令人放松的”，用来指物。
  Don't relax your efforts.
  你不要松懈下来。
  Don't worry about it, just try to relax.
  不要为这事担心，放松些。
  I felt rather relaxed after the examination.
  考试结束后，我感到很放松。
  We had a relaxing holiday.
  我们过了一个放松的假期。

9. just as： 就在……的时候
  He dashed to grasp her just as she stumbled over a stone.
  就在她被石头绊了一下的时候，他冲过去抓住了她。
  He came just as I was leaving.
  我正要走时他来了。

10. answer the phone： 接电话
  hung up the phone：挂断电话
  talk to somebody over the phone：在电话里和某人讲话
  make a phone call：打个电话

11. get along with： 进展，相处
  We get well along with each other.
  我们相互相处得很好。
  How do you get along with your work?
  你工作进展得如何？

12. Everything was ready except setting the table.
  什么都准备好了，就差摆桌子了。
  No one except me knew it.
  除我之外没人知道它。
  Every one of us, except him, went to watch the match.
  除他以外，我们都去观看了比赛。

13. reach the head of the stairs： 到达楼梯顶上
  They reached London on Saturday evening.
  他们于星期六晚上到达了伦敦。
  The heat of summer has reached its climax.
  已经是夏季最热的天气了。

14. be home for dinner： 回家吃晚饭
  I will be home in half an hour.
  我将在半小时内到家。
  We ran home for dinner.
  我们跑回家吃晚饭。

15. ask about the family： 问候全家人
  I only want to ask about the weather.
  我只想询问一下天气。

16. do some volunteer work： 做一些义务工作
  volunteer adj. & n. & v.
  volunteer social workers：志愿的工作人员
  hospital volunteers：医院志愿人员
  volunteer to give blood：义务献血

17. cleaning up the kitchen： 收拾，打扫厨房
  Your desk is in a mess; clean it up.
  你的书桌太乱了，整理一下。
  He hurried to clean up the pieces of the broken plates.
  他赶紧把盘碟的碎片打扫干净。

18. with a sigh： 叹了一口气
  "I wish I had finished this work," she said with a sigh.
  “我希望我完成了这项工作，”她叹口气说。

19. without even answering it： 甚至连电话都不接
  He left without even saying a word.
  他甚至不说一声就走了。
  They never meet without quarreling.
  他们一见面就要吵嘴。

20. called to somebody： 对……喊道
  She called to him for help.
  她向他喊叫求救。

21. get / be ready： 准备好
  Are you ready?
  你准备好了吗？
  I get ready for a journey.
  我准备旅行。

22. get away from： 逃离
  I couldn't get away from the office before 6.
  六点之前我不能离开办公室。
  The prisoners got away because of the police's carelessness.
  由于警察的疏忽，犯人逃走了。
