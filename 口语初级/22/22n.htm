1. If I don't do anything else： 哪怕我什么都不做（我也得……）
  If I don't do anything else, I will have to go to the supermarket.
  哪怕我什么都不做，我也得去趟超市。
  Would you like anything else?
  还想再要些别的吗?
  Be careful, or else you will make a mistake.
  小心，否则你会犯错的。

2. economics paper： 经济学论文
  Do you finish your English paper?
  你完成英语论文了吗？
  I think the paper is easy.
  我想卷子不难。

3. at his desk： 在他的桌子旁
  He sat at the table and read the newspaper.
  他坐在桌子旁看报纸。

4. look out of the window： 向窗外看
  look up / down：向上 / 下看
  looked to the right：向右看
  look at me：看着我
  look away：把脸转过去
  look back：回头看
  注意以上有关 look 的词组在上下文里的引申含义。

5. get ... dirty： 把……弄脏
  I got my arm broken.
  我把胳膊摔断了。
  He got the children tired and cross.
  他把孩子们弄得疲惫而且恼怒。

6. much too long： 太长了
  注意 much too 和 too much 的区别，搞清楚谁修饰谁的问题。
  She worries too much.
  她太担心了。
  She is much too fat.
  她太胖了。

7. the day of Uncle George's arrival： 乔治叔叔到来的那天
  The day of computer science is well upon us.
  计算机科技时代正向我们走来。

8. find one's way to： 找到去……的路
  I can't find my way to Mary's new house.
  我找不到去玛丽新家的路了。
  Can you tell me the way to the post office? I can't find my way.
  你能告诉我去邮局的路吗？我找不到路了。

9. give him specific directions： 给他仔细地指示方向
  I gave him full directions to enable him to find the house.
  我向他作了详细说明，好让他能找到那房子。

10. on the telephone： 在电话上
  You are wanted on the telephone.
  请你去接电话。

11. at the corner of Pine and State Street： 在 Pine 和 State 大街的拐角处
  The shop is situated at the corner of the street.
  商店位于街道拐角处。
  The table stood in the corner of the room.
  桌子在房间的角上。

12. turn left / right： 向左 / 右转弯
  Turn left at the corner.
  在拐角处向左拐。
  The school is on the left of the road.
  学校在马路的左边。

13. in the middle of the block： 在街区的中央
  Please stand in the middle of the room.
  请站在房子中间。
  I woke in the middle of the night.
  我半夜醒了。

14. That's it.
  对了。
  口语中经常会用到以 that 作主语的简单句，起到承上启下的作用。
  That's all.
  就说到这里。
  That gets me!
  可把我难住了！（可真烦死我了！）
