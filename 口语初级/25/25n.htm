1. wait patiently in the long line： 耐心地排在长队里
  Please stand in a line.
  请排队。
 
2. pick up： 捡起，偶然获得，购买
  Mother asked me to pick up the broken pieces of glass.
  妈妈让我把玻璃碎片捡起来。
  I picked up a mink coat on sale.
  我在拍卖时随手买了一件貂皮大衣。
  She picked up some beverages on the way home.
  她在回家的路上买了些饮料。

3. do (the) chores： 做家务
  Does your husband do the chores when he is at home?
  你丈夫在家的时候做家务吗？
  She finds shopping a chore.
  她认为买东西是烦人的事。

4. help (somebody) out： 帮助（某人）解决困难
  She's always willing to help me out when I am in trouble.
  我有麻烦时，她总是急人所难。

5. daydream： vi. & n. 幻想
  He sat in the classroom, daydreaming about his holiday by the sea.
  他坐在教室里幻想着海滨度假。
  She stared out of the window, lost in daydreams.
  她凝视窗外，沉浸在白日梦中。

6. be roughly shoved aside： 被猛推到一边
  Please help me to shove a boat into the water.
  请帮我把船推到水里。
  I was shoved in a room where I knew no one.
  我被推进了一间屋子，里面的人我一个也不认识。

7. live on his block： 与他住在同一街区
  I live two blocks from the school.
  我住在离学校两个路口的地方。

8. fly out of her hand： 从她手里飘落下来
  Clouds were flying across the sky.
  云飘过天空。
  I flew down the road.
  我在马路上飞跑。

9. fall at Sam's feet： 掉在萨姆的脚下
  He fell out the window.
  他从窗口掉下来。
  I've been on my feet all day.
  我已经站了一整天。
  I had to do everything at his feet.
  我做每件事都得受他控制。

10. criticize somebody for (doing) something： 责备某人做某事
  Mr. Black criticized Tom for breaking the glass.
  布莱克太太责备汤姆把杯子打碎了。
  He criticized the decision as unrealistic and the fact proved that he was right.
  他批评这个决定是不合实际的，事实证明他是对的。

11. belong to： 属于
  That pencil belongs to me.
  那支笔属于我。
  That dictionary belongs to the library.
  那本词典是图书馆的。

12. deserve： v. 值得
  He should live by himself and doesn't deserve our help.
  他应该靠自己过活，不值得我们帮助。
  If you do wrong, you deserve to be punished.
  你如做错事, 应当受罚。

13. Jack went out, but Colin had to stay at home.
  杰克出去了，可是科林只能待在家里。
  Xiao Zhang likes steamed bread, but she had to eat rice when she was on a business trip in Hunan last week.
  小张喜欢吃馒头，可她上星期到湖南出差的时候只能吃米饭。

14. look after： 照顾
  My friend looked after my dog while I was on holiday.
  在我去度假的时候，我的朋友照料我的狗。
  He's old enough to look after himself.
  他这么大了，已经能照顾自己了。

15. miss： vt 错过
  I was late because I missed the bus.
  我迟到了，因为我误了公共汽车。
  I felt chagrined at missing my train by three minutes.
  我差三分钟未赶上火车，为此感到很懊恼。
