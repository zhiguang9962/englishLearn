1. in one's leisure time： 在闲暇时间
  What do you do in your leisure time?
  你空闲时间做些什么？
  I am very busy and have no leisure to go to the cinema with you.
  我很忙，没有空闲时间和你看电影了。

2. be carried away： 使沉溺于，使着迷
  He has been carried away by desire.
  他已经被欲望冲昏了头脑。
  He tends to get carried away when watching the football match on TV.
  他一看电视中的足球比赛就很兴奋。

3. as usual： 像往常一样
  As usual, I slept late that Saturday morning.
  和平时一样，那个星期六早晨我起得很晚。

4. make noises instead of music： 制造噪音，而不是音乐
  Who's making those strange noises?
  谁弄出的那种怪声？
  Don't make so much noise.
  别那么吵闹。
  We'll go to Qingdao for our holiday instead of Dalian.
  我们将会去青岛度假，而不是大连。

5. be absorbed： 全神贯注
  He is absorbed in study.
  他专心读书。
  I was absorbed in the book and forgot my date with her.
  我被这本书所吸引，忘了和她的约会。

6. pay attention to somebody / something： 注意某人 / 某事
  You should pay more attention to state affairs.
  你应该多关心国家大事。
  You never pay attention to me whatever I did.
  无论我做什么，你从来都不注意我。

7. a worn-out shoe： 一只穿破的鞋子
  a worn-out coat：一件破烂不堪的大衣
  a worn-out age：风蚀残年

8. This was not the place for him.
  这不是他能待的地方。
  City streets are no place to be if you don't like noise or crowds.
  要是怕吵，怕人多就别上街。
  The classroom is no place for you to be left alone at night.
  教室不是你晚上独自待的地方。

9. be badly hurt： 深深地受到伤害
  She's hurt because you haven't visited her.
  她很伤心，因为你没有去看她。
  I was very much hurt at his words.
  他的话伤透了我的心。

10. appreciate one's works： 欣赏某人的作品
  Do you appreciate good tea?
  你会鉴赏好茶吗？
  She taught me how to appreciate the English poetry.
  她教我如何欣赏英语诗。

11. There was no other sound except the church toll.
  除了教堂的钟声，没有其他声音。
  There is no other student except Tom in the classroom.
  教室里除了汤姆没有其他学生。
  I have no other shoes except this pair.
  除了这双鞋，我没有其他可穿的鞋了。

12. do one's best to do something： 尽某人最大努力做某事
  I'll do my best to persuade her to come.
  我会尽我最大努力说服她来。
  We'll do our best to finish the task.
  我们会尽最大努力完成任务。

13. be inspired： 得到灵感，受到鼓舞
  I was inspired to study harder than ever before.
  我受到激励，比以往任何时候都更加努力学习。
  He is an artist who was inspired by Impressionism.
  他是一位受印象派鼓舞的艺术家。

14. stretch out from the grave： 从坟墓中伸出来
  He stretched out a helping hand friendly.
  他友好地伸出了一只援助之手。
  I stretched out my arm to take the book.
  我伸出手臂去拿书。

15. give ... a kick： 踢……一脚
  If the door won't open, give it a kick.
  如果门打不开的话，就踢它一脚。
  He was so angry that he gave me a heavy kick.
  他特别生气，重重地踢了我一脚。

16. His works was not accepted by anyone, not even the dead.
  没有人愿意接受他的作品，甚至是死人。
  I've received an invitation from him, but I'm not going to accept it.
  我收到了他的邀请，但是我不准备接受。
  Nobody will accept your theory.
  没有人会接受你的理论。

17. empty： v. 倒空
  The office was emptied very quickly.
  这办公室很快就腾空了。
  No waste or garbage is to be emptied on the street.
  废物和垃圾不许倒在街道上。

18. at the back of the school： 在学校后面
  I sat at the back of the classroom and couldn't hear clearly.
  我坐在教室后排，听不清。
  He drove his bus into the back of a car.
  他驾驶公共汽车撞上了一辆小车的车尾。
