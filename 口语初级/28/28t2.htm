Don't Throw Paper on the Floor



    Bill Davidson is throwing some paper on the floor. Miss Mead, his teacher, is looking at the paper.
MISS MEAD: Don't throw paper on the floor, Bill.
BILL: Where shall I put it, miss?
MISS MEAD: Put it in the waste-paper basket, please.
BILL: But Peter and Tony put all their paper in the basket a few moments ago. Now it's full.
MISS MEAD: In that case, take the basket outside and empty it.
BILL: Yes, miss.
    (He takes the basket out of the room and returns with it a few moments later.)
MISS MEAD: Where did you empty the basket, Bill?
BILL: In the playground, miss.
MISS MEAD: Silly boy! The dustbin is at the back of the school. Now pick up the paper and put it in the dustbin.
