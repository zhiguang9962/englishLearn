1. There was one sausage left over.
  还剩下一根香肠。
  I had the rice left over from yesterday.
  我有昨天剩下的米饭。
  We finished everything and there was nothing left over at all.
  我们做完了所有的事情，一点都没落下。

2. care for： 喜欢
  I don't care for coffee.
  我不喜欢喝咖啡。
  He cares for her very much.
  他非常喜欢她。

3. eat up： 吃光
  Eat up the fried chicken before it gets cold.
  把这块炸鸡吃掉，别让它凉了。
  He ate up all the food on the table ten minutes later.
  10分钟后，他吃光了桌子上所有的食物。

4. shake one's head： 摇头（通常表示持不赞同意见）
  shake one's fist：挥动拳头
  shake hands：握手

5. stand up properly： 站稳
  behave properly：举止得体
  properly speaking (= speak properly)：严格地说

6. He has eaten something that didn't agree with him.
  他吃了一些不合适（不好消化）的东西。
  Spicy food does not agree with her.
  辛辣食物不合她的胃口。
  The liquor did not agree with me.
  这酒我喝不了。

7. I had a very unpleasant hour with the doctor.
  我在医生那儿的一个钟头折腾得很厉害。
  Did you have a good time with her?
  你和她处得愉快吗？
  We had a difficult time last winter because Jack lost his job.
  我们去年冬天日子不好过，因为杰克失业了。

8. seem alright： 似乎没事
  The child seems healthy, but I am still concerned.
  这个孩子看来好像很健康，但我仍旧很焦虑。
  It seems like rain.
  好像要下雨。
  Alright, let's go swimming.
  好吧，我们去游泳。

9. jump up at ...： 跳起来冲向……
  The cat jumped up at the mouse.
  猫向老鼠扑过去。
  He jumped up into the chair.
  他跳起来坐到椅子上。

10. Which of the two do you think is better?
  你认为这两台哪一台好一些？
  Which of you will go with me?
  你们哪一个跟我一起去?
  Which of the three books do you like best?
  这三本书你最喜欢哪一本？
 
11. What's the difference between them?
  它们之间有什么区别？
  In fact there is not much difference between them.
  实际上，它们之间没有太大区别。
  You should learn the difference between good and evil.
  你应该学会区分善与恶。

12. be made of： 用……造成（通常指制造后不改变该材料原来的性质）
  注意 be made of 只限于物理变化，而 be made from 针对于化学变化。
  The bed is made of wood and steel.
  这床是由木头和钢铁造成的。
  Wine is made from grapes.
  葡萄酒是用葡萄酿制的。

13. The other one is good for the money.
  另外一台价钱比较适宜。
  Doing morning exercises is good for health.
  做早操对健康有益。
  The pork liver is good for your eyes.
  猪肝对眼睛有好处。

14. be satisfied with： 对……满意
  I was very satisfied with the service at that hotel.
  我对那家旅馆的服务很满意。
  I felt quite satisfied after my big meal.
  我这顿饭吃得很多，觉得很饱。

15. by cheque： 用支票付款
  bad cheque / rubber cheque / dishonoured cheque：空头支票
  by cash：现金支付
  by credit card：信用卡支付
