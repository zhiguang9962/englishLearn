1. the radio news： 广播新闻
  foreign news：国外消息
  home news：国内消息
  news to this hour：最新消息

2. a pack of hungry dogs： 一群饿狗
  a pack of matches：一盒火柴
  a pack of wolves：一群狼
  a pack of cards：一副纸牌
  a pack of books：一捆书

3. the old fellow： 这个老家伙
  fellow worker：同事
  schoolfellow：同学
  fellow countryman：同胞，同乡

4. here and there： 各处，到处
  Jack threw the paper here and there on the floor.
  杰克把纸扔到地上到处都是。
  It is covered with camellia every here and there on the hill.
  山上各处都开满了山茶花。

5. push open a matchbox： 推开一个火柴盒
  They pushed the door open.
  他们把门推开。
  Don't push against me!
  别推我！

6. hold something in one's hand： 手里拿着某物
  She was holding two books in her hand.
  她手里拿着两本书。
  That woman held a baby in her arm.
  那女人怀里抱着一个婴儿。

7. pay one's bill： 付账
  pay a bill for a room：付房租
  pay the telephone bill：付电话费
  pay the electricity bill：付电费

8. trouble somebody with something： 因为某事麻烦某人
  Don't trouble him with such trifles.
  不要因这等小事去麻烦他。
  May I trouble you to close the door?
  能麻烦您关下门吗?
  I am troubled with a headache.
  我头疼得挺厉害。

9. in the bottom of the matchbox： 在火柴盒的底部
  Some sugar left in the bottom of your cup.
  你杯底剩下一些糖。
  They found something strange in the bottom of the sea.
  他们在海底发现了某种奇怪的东西。

10. on a Sunday afternoon： 在一个星期天的下午
  注意 on a Sunday afternoon 和 on Sunday afternoon 的区别：前者是泛指，
  后者是特指“这个星期天下午”。
  on Monday evening：在星期一晚上
  on the morning of December 16：在十二月十六日的早晨
 
11. Nice to hear from you again.
  很高兴又接到你的电话。
  Nice to meet you.
  认识你很高兴。
  I hear from Mary every month.
  我每月都接到玛丽的信。

12. go away on holiday： 外出度假
  "When are you going away on holiday?" "Not until next month."
  “你打算什么时候外出度假？”“要到下个月。”
  I was on holiday in Spain at this time last year. I enjoyed my trip there.
  我去年这个时候在西班牙度假。我在那儿的旅行很愉快。

13. Will the party go on very late?
  晚会要到很晚吗？
  He got here three minutes late.
  他到这里迟了三分钟。
  I saw her as late as yesterday.
  直到昨天我还看见过她。

14. You don't have to stay that long.
  你没必要呆那么长时间。
  that 在这里作副词。
  I like her but not all that much.
  我喜欢她，但远不是那么着迷。
  You needn't take what he said that seriously.
  你没必要那么认真地接受他的话。

15. Come any time after eight.
  八点以后什么时候来都可以。
  Any time from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  从上午八点到下午五点什么时候都可以。
  You can select any one among these items.
  你可以从这几条中任选一条。
