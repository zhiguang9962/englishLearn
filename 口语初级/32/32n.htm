1. a pocketful of pigs： 一袋猪
  a pocketful of coins：一袋硬币
  a bag of corn：一袋玉米
  bags of time：大量时间
  bags of money：许多钱

2. This is the way it used to be.
  从前就是这样的。
  This is the way I cook vegetables.
  我就是这样做蔬菜的。
  This is the way he speaks.
  他就是这样说话。

3. give you my cow for your pig： 用我的母牛换你的猪
  I gave him a pound for his help.
  我付给他一英镑作为帮忙的酬金。
  I gave him a book for three dollars.
  我以三美元的价格卖给他一本书。

4. trade： v. & n. 交易
  She traded her books for his dictionary.
  她拿书换了他的字典。
  Would you mind trading seats with me?
  你介意和我换一下位子吗？
  Since joining the WTO, our trade with the foreign countries has greatly increased.
  自从加入世贸组织，我们与国外的贸易大增。

5. work out： 作出，设计出
  Can you work out this equation?
  你能解出这道方程式吗？
  First of all, you should work out a plan.
  首先，你应该制定出一个计划。

6. come out even： 结果公平
  Everything came out wrong.
  每件事结果都是错误的。
  It came out that woman had been telling a pack of lies.
  结果是那个女人一直在说谎。
  an even fight：一场势均力敌的打斗
  an even bargain：公平的交易
  an even chance of winning：具有同等获胜的机会

7. carry around： 随身带着
  I left here desperately, carrying around all my things.
  我失望地离开了这里，带着我所有的家当。
  I never carry much money with me.
  我身上从来不多带钱。
  He was carried away on a stretcher.
  用担架把他抬走了。

8. use / spend too much time doing something： 花费太多时间做某事
  Don't spend too much time doing such a trifle.
  不要在这么一件小事身上花费太长时间。
  He used all his money to buy a car.
  他用他所有的钱买了一辆汽车。

9. think of： 考虑，想到，想起
  I am thinking of going to America.
  我正考虑去美国。
  Can you think of any explanation for his absence from the yesterday's meeting?
  他昨天会议缺席，你能想得出有什么理由进行解释吗？
  What do you think of his paintings?
  你觉得他的画怎么样？

10. stand for： 代表，代替
  "OEM" stands for Original equipment manufacturer.
  “OEM”是“Original equipment manufacturer”（原始设备制造商）的缩写形式。
  I hate fascism and all it stands for.
  我痛恨法西斯主义及其代表的一切。

11. watch somebody doing something： 看某人做某事
  注意 watch somebody doing something 与 watch somebody do something 的区别：
  前者是看着某人正在做某事，无须注视全过程；后者是注视全过程。
  I watched the children crossing the road.
  我看着孩子们横过马路。
  I watched the children cross the road.
  我看着孩子们横过了马路。

12. That's all right.
  好吧。
  Your answers are all right.
  你的答案是正确的。
  "Let's go to school together." "All right."
  “我们一起上学去吧。”“好吧。”

13. finish doing something： 做完某事
  Did you finish cleaning the room?
  你打扫完房间了吗？
  I have finished (doing) my homework.
  我已经做完作业了。
