1. have lunch： 吃午饭
  have some tea：喝茶
  have coffee：喝咖啡
  have a sandwich for dinner：吃块三明治作为晚饭

2. make some tea： 泡点茶
  She made coffee for all of us.
  她给我们大家煮了咖啡。
  He made himself a cup of tea.
  他自己沏了一杯茶。

3. hear about： 听说
  I heard about your accident.
  我听说你出车祸的事了。
  I have ever heard about that girl.
  我曾经听说过那个女孩。

4. I'm not surprised Malcolm isn't well.
  马尔科姆身体不适，我一点也不奇怪。
  well 在这里作形容词，指身体健康。
  She got well soon.
  她很快恢复了健康。
  All is well.
  一切都尽如人意。

5. have very little money： （他）几乎没有钱
  此处 little 表示否定语气，意思是“少，一点点”。
  You eat very little.
  你吃得很少。
  I have too little time to finish this work.
  我的时间太少了，不能完成这项工作。

6. a room over a restaurant： 在饭馆上面的一间小屋
  There is a sign over the door. It says "I'm not in."
  门上方有个标记，上面写着“我不在家。”
  They built a bridge over the river.
  他们在河上架了一座桥。

7. live in a poor room like this： 住在一间像这样的破旧屋子里
  live in a town：住在一座城镇上
  live on a farm：住在农场里
  live at the seaside：住在海滨
  She worked like hell in order to earn more money.
  为了多赚钱，她拼命工作。

8. be busy with (doing) something： 忙于（做）某事
  I am busy myself with answering letters.
  我忙于回信。
  What are you busy with?
  你在忙什么呢？

9. be interested in (doing) something： 对某事感兴趣
  She is not interested in math.
  她对数学不感兴趣。
  Now I am only interested in marrying Mary.
  现在我只对和玛丽结婚的事情感兴趣。
  I'm interested to hear about you and your boy friend.
  我很想听听你和你男朋友的情况。

10. plain rice： 白米饭
  plain talk：开诚不公的交谈
  plain water：纯净水
  a plain man：平凡的人
  a plain face：普通的面孔（暗指长相难看）

11. take somebody to court： 上法庭告某人
  He took her to court for repayment of the debt.
  他为索取债务而起诉她。
  The killer was brought to court for trial.
  杀人犯被提到法庭受审。
 
12. accuse somebody of doing something： 控告某人做某事
  They accused him of taking bribes.
  他们控告他受贿。
  She was accused as an accomplice.
  她被指控为同谋犯。

13. take somebody / something seriously： 严肃对待某人 / 某事
  Don't take it so seriously.
  别把这件事看得这么严重。
  Do you take me for a fool?
  你以为我是个傻瓜吗？

14. complain to the court： 向法庭申诉
  You have the right to complain to the court, but you must afford the legal fare if you lose.
  你有权向法庭申诉，但是如果你输了，你必须承担诉讼费。
  She is always complaining about something.
  她总是满腹牢骚。
  He complained of a pain in his head.
  他说他头痛。

15. be guilty： 有罪，犯罪
  You are guilty of cheating.
  你犯了诈骗罪。
  He was found guilty.
  他被判有罪。
