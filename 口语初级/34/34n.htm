1. be sick with disappointment： 由于失望而懊丧
  with 在这里表示原因。
  jump with joy：高兴得跳起来
  shake with cold：冷得发抖
  smile with pleasure：高兴地微笑

2. turn out well： 结果很好
  The plan turned out a failure.
  这项计划结果归于失败。
  Things turned out to be exactly as we had expected.
  事情正如我们所预料的那样。

3. give up： 放弃
  You should give up smoking.
  你应该戒烟。
  I have given up my job since I got married.
  自从结婚后，我就放弃了工作。
  He doesn't give up easily.
  他做任何事都不轻易放弃。

4. make somebody proud of ...： 令某人为……而骄傲
  My daughter made me proud of her.
  我的女儿令我为她感到骄傲。
  He is proud of his ability to speak four languages.
  他为自己能说四种语言而骄傲。

5. be gifted： 有天赋
  She is gifted with talents.
  她很有才能。
  He is a gifted pianist.
  他是一位有才华的钢琴家。

6. be anxious to do something： 渴望做某事
  I was anxious to meet you.
  我急切地想与你会面。
  She was anxious to please him.
  她很想讨好他。

7. as though (= as if)： 好像，仿佛
  He was walking as though he had hurt his leg.
  他走路的样子好像伤了腿似的。
  It looked as though they had been quarreling.
  他们看上去像是在吵架。

8. take a deep breath： 深吸一口气
  get one's breath back / catch one's breath：喘口气
  hold one's breath：屏住呼吸
  be short of breath：上气不接下气
  be out of breath：喘不过气来

9. in slow motion： 慢动作
  You must not get out of the car when it is in motion.
  汽车行驶的时候千万不能下车。
  He set the machine in motion.
  他使机器运转起来。

10. stage fright： 舞台恐惧症，怯场
  You gave me a fright.
  你吓了我一跳。
  I got the fright of my life.
  我吓得要命。

11. in ruins： 成为废墟，遭到严重破坏
  The house where I lived in the past is in ruins.
  我过去住的那栋房子已成废墟。
  We saw the ruins of the building.
  我们看见了这座建筑物的废墟。

12. by air (= by plane)： 乘飞机
  by sea：乘船
  by train：坐火车
  by bus：乘公共汽车

13. catch fire： 着火
  The forest caught fire.
  森林着火了。
  He set on fire at the back of her house.
  他在她家后面放了火。

14. put out： 扑灭，熄灭
  put out 在本文中作“扑灭（大火）”讲，但它在别的场合还有一些其他解释，必须根据上下文
  来理解其正确语意。
  He behaved bravely when he put out a fire.
  在扑灭大火的时候他表现得很勇敢。
  Please put out the light before you go to bed.
  上床之前请把灯关掉。
  He was put out first in the match.
  他在比赛中首先被淘汰出局。

15. the emergency runway： 紧急跑道
  emergency door：太平门
  emergency fund：应急基金
  emergency meeting：紧急会议
