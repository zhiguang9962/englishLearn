1. expect somebody to do something： 期望某人做某事
  My parents expect me to be successful.
  我父母期望我成功。
  You are expected to finish the work in time.
  希望你按时完成工作。

2. an average eleven-year-old kid： 一个平常的11岁的孩子
  He is a student of average intelligence.
  他属于智力一般的学生。
  I went to visit her once a week on average.
  我平均一周去看她一次。

3. I'm average, too, when it comes to basketball, football, soccer, and baseball.
  而且打篮球，踢橄榄球、足球，还有打棒球的时候我也很平常。
  When it comes to politics, I know nothing.
  我对政治一无所知。

4. be good at (doing) something： 擅长做某事
  He is very good at French.
  他精通法语。
  Are you good at cooking?
  你做饭好吃吗？

5. captain of the football team： 橄榄球队队长
  在美语中，football 是橄榄球，soccer 是足球。注意 captain 前面一般不需要用冠词
  the，这里表示作为队长的惟一性，后面的 editor of the school newspaper 也是一样的。
  Mr. Charles was elected chairman of the municipal education committee.
  查尔斯先生当选为市教育委员会主席。

6. tell you the truth： 实话对你讲
  To tell you the truth, your plan is very attractive, but it doesn't seem to be practicable.
  说实话，你的计划很吸引人，不过似乎行不通。

7. dream about (doing) something： 梦想做某事
  I dream about being a millionaire.
  我梦想成为百万富翁。
  Last night I dreamed of meeting an old friend.
  昨天晚上我梦见了一个老朋友。

8. impress somebody： 给某人留下印象
  His eyes impressed me deeply at first sight.
  我第一眼见到他时，他的眼睛给我留下了深刻的印象。
  I was impressed by the child's sincerity and gave him a few coins.
  我被那孩子的诚挚所感动，给了他几个铜板。

9. rescue a child from a burning building： 从燃烧的大楼中救出小孩
  He defied danger and rescued that girl from bandits.
  他不顾危险，从匪徒手中救出了那个女孩。

10. chase a robber away from an old lady： 把抢老太太钱的强盗赶走
  We had tried everything to chase those fears from her mind, but we failed.
  我们已经试过所有的方法来驱散她心中的恐惧，但是失败了。
  He asked me to chase the dog out of the garden.
  他叫我把狗赶出花园。

11. an instant cure for cancer： 速效治癌药
  Aspirin is a wonderful cure for colds.
  阿斯匹林是治感冒的妙药。
  Is there a certain cure for this disease yet?
  这种病迄今有无有效的治疗方法？

12. a shot for mental illness： 治精神病的针剂
  take a shot at the enemy：朝敌人开枪
  have a shot at this problem：设法解决这一问题
  an action shot of a dog chase：狗追赶时的连续镜头

13. Father's Day： 父亲节（每年6月的第三个星期日）
  Mother's Day：母亲节（每年5月的第二个星期日）
  (Saint) Valentine's Day：圣瓦伦廷节，情人节（每年2月14日）

14. PTA (= Parent-Teacher Association)： 家长与教师协会

15. start out： 起头，开始
  When are you going to start out?
  你打算什么时候动身？
  We started out on a hike on Sunday morning.
  星期天早晨我们开始远足。

16. see somebody as ...： 把某人看作是……
  I don't see him as a teacher, but a friend.
  我不把他当老师看待，而是当成朋友。
  Many people see him as a hero.
  许多人把他看作是英雄。

17. in the dark： 黑暗中
  All the lights went out and I was left in the dark.
  所有的灯都关了，我周围一片黑暗。
  You must come back before dark.
  你必须在天黑前回来。

18. surprise somebody with something： 用某物令某人吃惊
  You surprise me!
  真想不到！
  It wouldn't surprise me if you lost.
  你要是失败了也不足为奇。

19. have a bad allergy： 过敏很重
  Do you have an allergy to penicillin?
  你对青霉素有过敏反应吗？
 
20. bother somebody a lot： 严重困扰某人
  Don't bother me while I am writing.
  我写作的时候不要打扰我。
  You needn't bother with trifles.
  你无须为小事而烦恼。

21. I bet： 我断定，我敢说
  I bet you are wrong.
  我敢说是你错了。
  "They have married." "You bet?"
  “他们已经结婚了。” “当真？”

22. at the same time： 同时
  I don't like him, but admire him for his courage at the same time.
  我不喜欢他，但同时又佩服他的勇气。
  I will come to visit you again at the same time tomorrow.
  我明天这个时间再来看你。

23. call my essay "My Father's Son"： 给我的文章起名为“我父亲的儿子”
  Just call me Jimmy.
  叫我吉米好了。
  What will you call the baby?
  你们将给这孩子取什么名字？

24. Nobel Prize： 诺贝尔奖
  Nobel Laureate：诺贝尔奖金获得者
  Nobel Prize for Peace / Literature / Physics：诺贝尔和平奖 / 文学奖 / 物理学奖

25. He's been with the company only two years.
  他在公司里只干了两年。
  I've been with him for three years.
  我已经和他相处了三年。
  She is with a publishing company as an editor.
  她受雇于一个出版公司，做编辑。

26. What sort of clothes does he wear?
  他穿什么样的衣服？
  He's a sort of people who you'd better never believe in.
  他是那种你最好永远也不要相信的人。
  She is the sort of girl that Geoffrey loves.
  她正是杰弗里所喜欢的那种女孩。

27. have something to do with ...： 与……有关
  I think he must have something to do with this matter.
  我想他一定与这件事情有关。
  I swear I had nothing to do with selling drugs.
  我发誓我与贩卖毒品无关。
