1. go to her doctor： 去看医生
  Mary went to her mother that morning.
  玛丽那天早晨去找她母亲了。
  We are going to our parents for the weekend.
  我们打算与父母共度周末。

2. give somebody trouble： 麻烦人家
  Jim caused a lot of trouble to his parents；he was always in trouble.
  吉姆给他的父母招来很多麻烦，他甚为苦恼。
  I went to a lot of trouble to find this book.
  我费了好大劲才找到这本书

3. listen to： 听
  Listen to me carefully.
  认真听我说。
  I listen to the radio every morning.
  我每天早晨收听广播。

4. stop smoking： 戒烟
  注意 stop doing something 与 stop to do something 的区别：前者是“停止做某事”，后者是
  “停下来做另一件事”。另外，stop somebody (from) doing 表示“阻止某人做某事”。
  She stopped crying.
  她不哭了。
  We stopped to rest.
  我们停下来休息。
  They stopped me going out of the door.
  他们阻止我出门。

5. You'll soon be quite all right.
  你很快就会好的。
  Are you all right?
  你好吗？（你没事吧？）
  "I'm sorry." "That's all right."
  “对不起。”“没什么。”

6. like smoking： 喜欢抽烟
  like 作为动词使用时，注意 like doing something 和 like to do something 的区别：
  前者表示一种长期的爱好，后者只限于某一时刻或某一场合的喜好。
  I like swimming.
  我喜欢游泳。
  I don't like to trouble you.
  我不想麻烦你。

7. not ... any more： 不再……
  Don't eat any more ice cream, or you will have a stomachache.
  不要再吃冰淇淋了，否则你会肚子疼的。
  Is there any more water in the glass?
  杯子里还有水吗？

8. at once： 马上
  Do it at once.
  马上做。
  Don't all speak at once!
  大家别一块儿说话！

9. think for a few seconds： 想了一会儿
  等同于 think for a minute。注意 a minute / a few seconds 在英语口语中的使用。
  Wait a minute, please!
  请等一会儿。
  I'll arrive in a minute.
  我马上就到。

10. fried potatoes： 油炸土豆片

11. say goodbye to somebody： 对某人说再见
  We shall visit his picture show and then we shall say goodbye to him.
  我们将参观他的画展，然后与他告别。

12. get up： 起床
  I usually get up at six o'clock every day.
  我通常每天六点起床。
  He is still asleep; he hasn't got up.
  他还在睡觉，没起床。

13. a bad stomachache： 肚子很痛
  a bad cold：重感冒
  a bad piece of news：一条令人不快的新闻
  a bad temper：坏脾气

14. call somebody： 给某人打电话
  Please call me after nine.
  请九点之后给我打电话。
  I called twice, but no one answered.
  我打了两次电话，但无人应答。

15. get out of bed： 下床，起床
  I decided to get out of my bad habits.
  我决定改掉我的坏毛病。
  Get out of it!
  [俚]别胡扯啦！

16. Operator, give me Spring 3-2546, please.
  接线员，请给我接Spring 3-2546。
  打电话要求接线员转接时，常用 give me + telephone number 的句式。

17. keep somebody in bed： 让某人卧床
  The doctor attempted to keep the patient calm.
  医生试图使病人镇静。
  Keep the children away from the hot stove.
  别让孩子走近热炉子。

18. 44 Washington Avenue： 华盛顿大街44号
  注意英语中地址的写法通常是将门牌号一类的数字写在某街或某路的前面。
