1. put on weight： 增加体重
  lose weight：减轻体重

2. give up： 放弃
  He told me that he had given up playing football.
  他告诉我他已经不再踢足球了。
  She was forced to give up school when her father died.
  父亲去世后，她被迫辍学。
  I will never give up my ideas.
  我永远不会放弃我的想法。

3. do some good： 起作用，有好处
  Does it do any good arguing about it?
  辩论这个问题有什么好处吗？
  She said the meeting would do no good.
  她说开这个会没有什么好处。

4. have common sense： 懂常识
  have a keen sense of hearing：听觉灵敏
  have much sense of humor：很有幽默感
  You haven't the sense to come in out of the rain?
  你不知道进来避雨吗？
  There's a lot of sense in what he says.
  他说的话很有见地。

5. be of average height and weight： 中等身材，不胖不瘦

6. get out of bed： 起床

7. except for： 除……之外
  I can answer all the questions except for the last.
  除了最后一道，所有的问题我都能回答。
  The meal was excellent except for the first course.
  这顿饭好极了，只是第一道菜稍差。
  Your report is correct except that some details are omitted.
  除了有些细节未提到之外，你的报导是正确的。

8. All day at work I fight this tired feeling, just dragging myself around.
  我在一整天的工作中强打精神，在疲倦中苦苦挣扎。
  He dragged himself along.
  他拖着疲惫的身体前行。
  I dragged myself out of bed, still half asleep.
  我挣扎着起了床，犹自睡眼惺忪。

9. the front page： 头版

10. fall asleep： 睡着
  Don't wake him up ― he's sound asleep.
  别吵醒他――他睡得很香。
  Soon he fell into a deep sleep.
  一会儿他就熟睡过去。

11. the key to your problem： 你的问题的关键
  Diet and exercise are the key to good health.
  控制饮食和做运动是身体健康的关键。
  The key to the whole affair was her jealousy.
  整个事情的症结是因为她嫉妒。

12. stay up so late： 熬夜到很晚
  I stayed up nights studying for the final examinations.
  为了准备期末考试，我好几个晚上开夜车学习。
  He insisted on staying up to nurse me.
  他坚持要熬夜照顾我。

13. account for： 解释，说明
  Her illness accounts for her absence.
  她因病缺席。
  I hope you could account for your conduct.
  我希望你能为你的行为作出解释。

14. get out of the habit： 改掉习惯
  get into the habit：养成习惯

15. engage in： 使从事，参加
  We also engage in various kinds of sideline production.
  我们也从事各种副业生产。
  In those days they were not free to engage in patriotic activity.
  那时候我们没有参加爱国活动的自由。

16. avoid doing something： 避免做某事
  He always tried to avoid answering me.
  他总是设法避免回答我的问题。
  You must try to avoid repeating these errors.
  你不要再犯这些错误。
