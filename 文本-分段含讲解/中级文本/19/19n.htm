1. a grammar school： 英国的一种公立中学，主要目标是为高等院校输送学生
  a secondary modern school：英国自1944年成立的一种中学，学生不升入大学或其它高等院校
  a comprehensive system of schooling：综合性的教育体制，即grammar school 和secondary modern school 的综合体
  "O" levels：即Ordinary level，原为英国中学会考中最低的一级，1988年为GCSE所替代

2. be divided off： 被分开
  This part of the field was divided off with a fence, to keep the cows in.
  这块地用篱笆隔开，以防牛跑出去。

3. separate off： 分隔，（使）隔开
  Part of the office is separated off for the use of the manager.
  办公室的一部分隔开来供经理使用。

4. tend to do something： 倾向于做某事
  I tend to go to bed earlier during the winter.
  我在冬天常睡得较早。
  Women tend to live longer than men.
  女人多比男人长寿。

5. at the top end of： 处于顶尖位置
  at the lower levels of：处于下游水平

6. pass over： 忽略，不管（本课中的意思）；原谅，不追究；去世
  We may as well pass over the details.
  细节我们不妨略而不谈。
  She passed over his error.
  她原谅了他的错误。
  The old woman passed over this morning.
  那位老太太是今天早晨去世的。

7. be adapted to： 适应，适合
  The cleaner is particularly well adapted for use in the home.
  这种清洁器特别适合在家中使用。
  These plants are adapted to various climates, soils and other natural conditions.
  这些植物能适应各种气候、土壤和其它自然条件。

8. be much more aware of： 更加认识到
  It happened without me being aware of it.
  事情在我不知不觉中发生了。
  You are well aware of how much this means to me.
  你很清楚这对我多么重要。

9. in terms of： 在……方面，就……来说（本课中的意思）；以……为单位
  In terms of Sparkle Program training and self-construction, the center pays high attention to technique and personal quality training.
  在星火培训和自身建设方面，中心以技能培训和素质培训为重点。
  Distances are expressed in terms of kilometers as well as miles.
  公里和英里都用来表示距离。

10. consist of： 由……组成
  The book consists of eight chapters.
  全书共分八章。
  The apartment consisted of two bedrooms, a living-room and a kitchen.
  那套单元房包括两间卧室，一间起居室和一间厨房。

11. take up： 占用时间或空间（本课中的意思）；从事；开始学习或某项活动；提出，讨论
  I'm sorry to take up so much of your time.
  对不起，占了你这么多时间。
  When I left school I took up journalism.
  我离开学校后开始从事新闻工作。
  She took up the study of music at the age of six.
  她六岁就开始学音乐。
  Are you going to take the matter up with the Ministry?
  你打算把这件事向部里提出来吗？

12. in all： 总共
  There were ten of us in all for lunch.
  我们一共十个人吃午饭。
  That's $6.70 in all.
  总共6.70美元。

13. at this stage of adolescence： 在青春期这个阶段
  at an early stage in our history：在我们历史的早期

14. for one's own sake： 为了某人自己的好处
  I'll help you for your father's sake.
  看在你父亲的面子上，我会帮你。
  I did it for the sake of my family.
  我做这件事是为了我的家人。

15. be forced into： 迫使陷入某种状态
  They were forced into the battle at a place picked by our army.
  他们被迫在我军选定的地方作战。
  The president was forced into resigning.
  会长被迫辞职。

16. be fed up to the (back) teeth with： 对……感到厌烦透了
  I am fed up with his constant complaints.
  他经常抱怨，令我厌烦。
  The husband has been fed up with his wife's nagging.
  丈夫对妻子的唠叨感到厌烦。

17. equal to： 对等，相同（本课中的意思）；对某事物有力量、勇气、能力等
  One li is equal to half a kilometre.
  一华里等于半公里。
  He's equal to the occasion.
  他能应付这一局面。

18. be based on： 以……为基础、根据
  This novel is based on historical facts.
  这部小说是以历史事实为根据的。
  A democratic state is based on liberty.
  民主国家建立在自由的基础上。
