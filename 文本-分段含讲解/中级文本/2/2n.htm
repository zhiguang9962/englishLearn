1. au pair： 为了学习语言，不取报酬，吃住在雇主家，为雇主看管孩子、做家务的人
  We've got a Chinese au pair for three months.
  我们找到了一个可以干三个月的中国换工。

2. attend English classes： 上英语课
  She promised me that she would attend the show in the evening.
  她答应我晚上去看演出。
  The teacher suggested that I attend a technical school.
  老师建议我去上技校。
  How many courses do you attend this term?
  这学期你上几门课？

3. compare ... with ...： ……和……相比
  Compare this with that, and you will see which is better.
  把这个和那个比较一下，你就可以看出哪个好一些了。
  You shouldn't compare your merits with other's demerits.
  你不应该拿自己的优点和别人的缺点比。
  Overall industrial production was up by 20 per cent compared with last year.
  工业总产量和去年相比增长了20%。

4. get (be, become) used to something： 习惯于某事
  Are you used to this kind of weather?
  你习惯这种天气吗？
  You have to get used to hard work as quickly as possible, or you cannot work in this company.
  你必须尽快习惯艰苦的工作，否则你无法在这家公司就职。
  I'm not used to strong drink.
  我喝不了烈酒。

5. spend some time with somebody： 和某人度过一段时间
  He always spends weekends with his family.
  他总是和家人度周末。
  Tom spent a whole night with his friends.
  汤姆整晚都和朋友们在一起。
  I wish you would spend Christmas this year with me.
  我希望今年你能和我一起过圣诞节。

6. Head Teacher： 校长（“校长”通常是Head Master，Principal等）

7. get one's consent： 征得某人的同意
  Jane's parents refused their consent to her marriage.
  简的父母不同意她的婚事。
  I ought first to have asked your consent.
  我本应先征得你的同意。

8. prior to her departure： 在她离开前
  The contract will be signed prior to the ceremony.
  合同将在仪式举行之前签字。
  What did you do prior to coming here?
  你来这里之前做什么呢？
  It happened prior to my arrival.
  这事发生在我到达之前。

9. return fare： 往返车票
  single fare：单程票
  full fare：全价票
  half fare：半价票

10. expect ... of somebody： 期待，指望某人……
  I know from their greetings how much they expected of me.
  从他们对我热情的招呼中我可以看出，他们对我抱有多大的期望。
  I can't do it ― you expect too much of me.
  我做不了――你对我期望过高了。
  That is what the Party eagerly expects of us.
  这是党对我们的殷切期望。

11. shortly after： 之后立即
  Shortly after the accident the police arrived.
  事故发生后没多久，警察就赶到了。
  Shortly before noon he came to visit me.
  临近晌午，他来看我。

12. advertising executive： 广告商
  account executive：（广告或服务业的）业务经理，业务员
  financial executive：财务主管

13. the terms on which she would be employed： 她被雇用的条件
  I will not work for you on any terms.
  我决不会为你工作的。
  Would you like to accept it on these terms?
  你愿意接受这些条件吗？

14. a Public School in the Midlands： 米德兰的一所私立中学
  a Preparatory School in Surrey：萨里的一所私立小学
  the French Lycee：法国中学（在有的地方这种中学也包括学龄儿童，即从小学到高中。）

15. during term time： 在……期间
  We will have finished this book by the end of the spring term.
  到这个春季学期末我们将学完这本书。
  How about your end-of-term examinations?
  你期末考试考得怎么样？

16. make the beds： 铺床
  注意 make 后跟名词 bed 通常不是指“造床”，而是“铺床”。类似有引申含义的还有文中的
  lay the table（摆餐具）。

17. run errands： 跑腿办事
  I was tired of running errands for my brother.
  我已厌烦给哥哥当跑腿了。
  He tried to ask me to go on an errand, but I refused him.
  他想叫我跑一下腿，但我拒绝了。

18. pocket money： 零花钱

19. be delighted with something： 为某事而高兴
  My father was delighted with my article on Lu Xun.
  父亲为我写的关于鲁迅的那篇文章感到高兴。
  I was delighted with his success in business.
  我为他生意上的成功感到高兴。
