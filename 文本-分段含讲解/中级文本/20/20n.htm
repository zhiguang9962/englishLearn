1. get involved with： 给……缠住，牵涉，使参与
  She became involved with rackets.
  她涉嫌诈骗。
  I was involved with the Student Union as Treasurer.
  我曾在学生会当过一段时间的出纳。
  I don't feel inclined to get involved in this business.
  我不想卷入这件事。

2. keep up with the Joneses： 赶时髦
  可理解为邻居、朋友之间看别人买什么、做什么，自己就跟着也买什么、做什么，
  免得人家瞧不起自己。
  I had to run to keep up with him.
  我得跑着才跟得上他。
  We must keep up with the times.
  我们必须跟上时代。

3. feel like doing something： 想做某事
  I am tired and I feel like going to bed.
  我累了，想去睡觉。
  I don't feel like arguing with you.
  我不想和你争辩。

4. get bored with： 对……感到乏味
  I have got bored with this job.
  我对这件工作已经厌烦了。
  We are bored with his story.
  我们对他讲的故事感到厌倦。

5. kill time： 消磨时间
  kill a bottle of brandy：喝光一瓶白兰地
  kill a congressional bill：否决一项国会议案
  kill the pain：止痛

6. in one's opinion： 依某人看来
  In my opinion, you're wrong.
  依我看，你错了。
  In my opinion and in the opinion of most people, it is an impossible project.
  照我的和大多数人的看法，这是一项不可能实施的计划。

7. count on： 依靠，指望
  He is the only person I could count on.
  他是我惟一能指望的人。
  You can count on our support in any emergency.
  如果有什么紧急情况，我们一定支援。

8. fall in love with： 爱上
  fall out of love with：不爱
  After working together for many years, we fell in love with each other.
  我们在一起工作多年之后相爱了。
  I don't want to fall out with you about such trifles.
  我不想和你为这样的小事伤和气。

9. keep in touch with： 和……保持联系
  drift out of touch with：和……失去联系
  I try to keep in touch with current events by reading the newspapers.
  我通过看报纸来尽量了解时事。
  He is out of touch with current trends.
  他对现代潮流不闻不问。

10. get back into close contact： 恢复密切联系
  She comes into contact with many people.
  她和许多人有联系。
  We can learn much by being brought into contact with opposing opinions.
  通过接触反面意见，我们可以学到很多东西。

11. have sympathy for somebody： 同情某人
  That mean man had little sympathy for the homeless children.
  那个吝啬鬼对那些无家可归的孩子没什么同情心。
  I felt great sympathy for her when her mother died.
  她母亲去世时，我对她深为同情。

12. interfere in： 干涉，干预
  You have no right to interfere in other people's business.
  你无权干涉别人的事情。
  No one can interfere in this affair.
  谁也不能干预这件事。
