1. be justified to do something： 做某事被证明是正当的（be justified in doing something 更常用）
  He is fully justified in leaving the matter at that.
  他有充分的理由把事情只做到那一步为止。
  They were justified in turning down our proposal.
  他们没采纳我们的建议是有道理的。

2. cast away： 抛弃，丢掉（文中的意思）；浪费掉；使（船）失事
  Don't cast away these old papers.
  别把这些旧报纸扔掉。
  We were cast away by the storm on an island without food or water.
  我们被风暴刮到小岛上，没有食物，也没有淡水。

3. bear him a son： 给他生一个儿子
  His wife later bore a son and a daughter.
  他的妻子后来生了一男一女。
  Your hard work is bound to bear rich fruit.
  你的努力工作一定会结出丰硕的果实。

4. die hard： 难以根绝，顽固
  racial prejudices that die hard：很难消除的种族歧视
  radicalism that dies hard：顽固的激进分子

5. drop down in a faint： 昏倒
  go off in a faint：昏过去
  in a dead faint：完全失去知觉，昏死

6. give birth to： 生（孩子），使诞生
  The ewe gave birth to only one lamb.
  这母羊只产了一只羊羔。
  The Nanchang Uprising gave birth to the revolutionary army led by our Party.
  南昌起义使我党领导的革命军队诞生了。

7. to my mind： 我认为，我的意见是（本课中的意思）；合我的心意
  It is, to my mind, the greatest picture he ever painted.
  我认为这是他所画的作品当中最伟大的一幅。
  The arrangements he made were very much to my mind.
  他所作的安排很合我的心意。

8. by comparison： 通过比较
  This house costs more but is cheaper by comparison.
  这栋房子价格较高，但比较起来还是便宜的。
  Such differences are secondary by comparison with the overriding similarities.
  与占主要地位的相似点比较起来，这些差别是次要的。

9. carry on： 进行，继续
  We must carry on till success in spite of the extremely difficult conditions.
  尽管条件极端困难，我们必须坚持下去，直到成功。

10. turn out： 结果，原来（情况是）
  It looked like rain this morning, but it has turned out to be a fine day.
  今天早晨看起来要下雨，结果是个好天。
  It turned out that we failed.
  结果我们失败了。
  I'm sure it will turn out a futile attempt.
  我肯定这会白费功夫。

11. dig up： 翻挖，翻出
  We must dig up the weeds.
  我们应把野草挖掉。
  Where did you dig up the picture?
  这张照片你是从哪儿找出来的？

12. incur upon himself heavy fines for ...： 由于……他被罚了大笔的钱
  incur hatred：招致仇恨
  incur debts：负债
  incur a heavy loss：蒙受很大损失

13. spare no expense(s)： 不遗余力，不惜代价
  He spared no expense in helping me.
  他不惜代价地帮助我。
  We made up our minds to spare no efforts to scale the peaks in science and technology.
  我们决心全力攀登科技高峰。

14. beat up： 痛打一顿
  He charged in and started beating her up.
  他冲进来对她大打出手。
  They were like rats scampering in the street which everybody wants to beat up.
  他们像过街的老鼠人人喊打。

15. look people in the face： 正视人们
  lose face：丢脸
  laugh in his face：当面嘲笑他
  fly in the face of evidence：悍然不顾证据

16. load himself heavy with drink： 给他自己灌了很多酒
  a desk loaded with books：堆满书的桌子
  The novel is loaded with suspense.
  这部小说充满了悬念。
  They loaded him with gifts at his eighth birthday.
  在他八岁生日的时候，他们送了他很多礼物。

17. fend for oneself： 照料自己，自己谋生
  The seal pup must be taken back to the sea directly when she is able to fend for herself.
  一旦那只小海豹能独立生活，就必须把它放回海里去。
  We had two children to fend for.
  我们有两个孩子要抚养。

18. break the regulations on family planning： 违反计划生育的规定
  When anyone breaks the law, he should be punished.
  犯了法就应当受到制裁。
  He broke some rule of working the machinery.
  他违反了操作规程。

19. look down on [upon]： 看低，轻视
  The literary establishment generally looks down on artistes.
  文学界一般说来看不起画家。
  I don't know how you look upon this matter.
  我不知道你对这事怎么看。

20. pose the major barriers to limiting China's population： 成为限制中国人口增长的主要障碍
  The revolutionary movement poses a direct threat to their interests.
  革命运动对他们的利益造成了直接威胁。
  He sometimes poses difficulties for his parents.
  他有时给他父母带来种种麻烦。

21. the government's call for "marriage at mature ages"： 政府“晚婚”的号召
  The Prime Minister put out a call for greater effort.
  总理发出号召，要大家作更大的努力。
  I'm sorry to tell you nobody is going to answer your call for working overtime.
  我遗憾地告诉你，没有人打算响应你要求加班的号召。
