1. a discussion on the harmful effects of smoking： 有关吸烟有害的讨论
  a discussion about his work：对他的工作的讨论
  a question under discussion：讨论中的问题

2. take effective measures： 采取有效措施
  We must take strict measures against all forms of corruption.
  我们必须采取严厉措施对付任何形式的腐败行为。
  What steps are you going to take?
  你准备采取什么措施？

3. prevent somebody from doing something： 阻止某人做某事
  No force on earth can prevent them from winning final victory in their just struggle.
  世界上没有任何力量能阻止他们在这场正义斗争中取得最后胜利。
  The medicine can prevent you from catching a cold.
  这药能预防感冒。

4. make up： 构成，占（本课中的意思）；编写，捏造；弥补，补偿；化妆
  It made up one-third of my total income.
  它是我总收入的三分之一。
  Each student was asked to make up a dialogue.
  要求每个学生编一段对话。
  We'll do all we can to make up for the economic losses.
  我们要尽一切力量弥补这些经济损失。
  She was slightly made up.
  她化了点淡妆。

5. just out of curiosity： 只是出于好奇
  I said this out of kindness.
  我说这句话是出于好意。
  The new law came out of the government's concern over rising house prices.
  由于政府对房价上升的关注而产生了这项新法案。

6. speak in one voice against smoking： 异口同声地反对吸烟
  Does anyone give a voice in the matter?
  有人对此事发表意见吗？
  As no one raised his voice against the plan, it was agreed on.
  既然没有人反对，这项计划也就通过了。

7. liven ... up： 使……气氛活跃起来
  The variety of methods helps to liven up a lesson.
  教学方法多样化使教学更加生动。
  The place is really livening up with lots of new shops.
  这地方开了许多新店铺，真是热闹起来了。

8. something of an actress： 有几分表演天才
  He has something of the Slav temperament.
  他具有某些斯拉夫人的性格。
  The old man living in the apartment next to ours is something of an eccentric.
  住在我们隔壁房间的那个老头性情多少有点儿古怪。

9. contain oneself： 自制
  You should contain your tendency to argue.
  你应该克制好争辩的习性。

10. mutter under one's breath： 低声说
  splutter with rage：勃然大怒，气急败坏地说

11. rally round： 团结在……的周围，支持……
  We rallied round to give him support.
  我们大家站在他一边，给他支持。
  They made an appeal to party loyalists to rally round the flag.
  他们要求党内坚贞分子团结在党的旗帜周围。

12. be in uproar： 吵闹声一片
  in a mass：乱成一团
  in chaos and disorder：杂乱无章

13. show some sense： 有点理智
  He showed his good business sense.
  他显示了他的经济头脑。
  He showed great patience towards her.
  他对她很有耐心。

14. make a move： 做出反应，采取行动（本课中的意思）；动一下；（朝……）走去；走棋
  He didn't make any move when being asked about his wife.
  当问及妻子的情况时，他没有做出任何反应。
  If you make a move, I'll shoot you!
  你要是动一下，我就毙了你！
  She made a move for the window.
  她朝窗户走去。

15. play an important part in： 起着重要作用，扮演重要角色
  We must enable our staff to play their parts to the full.
  我们应当让员工充分发挥他们的作用。
  He played a shameful role in this matter.
  他在这件事中扮演了一个可耻的角色。

16. derive great pleasure out of ...： 从……中获得巨大乐趣
  derive pleasure from reading：从读书中获得乐趣
  derive a conclusion from facts：从事实推出结论

17. a hardened habit with somebody： （成了）某人的固习
  be of a thrifty habit：生性节俭
  a cheerful habit of mind：开朗的性格
  The habit has grown on him.
  他已经养成了那习惯。
  Sarcasm is a habit with her.
  她惯于讽刺挖苦别人。

18. give much thought to ...： 好好想想……
  He gave the subject a lot of thought.
  他对那题目想得很多。
  In fact I never give much thought to what she said.
  实际上，我从不在乎她说什么。

19. warn against： 告诫反对……
  Have you been warned against doing that?
  有人告诫你不要这样做吗？
  He warned me against being over-confident.
  他告诫我不要过于自信。

20. have no effect on： 对……无影响，不起作用
  Her happiness had great effect on the students in the classroom.
  她的快乐强烈地感染了教室里的学生们。
  Unfortunately my protest had no effect.
  不幸的是我的抗议无济于事。

21. run the risk of： 冒……的危险
  If he still insists, he will run the risk of being fired.
  如果他继续坚持的话，会有被解雇的危险。
  You were running a serious risk in doing a thing like this.
  做这样一件事，你是冒了极大风险的。
