1. hardly a few scores of yards： 才几十码
  Hardly anybody likes her, because she's so proud.
  几乎没有人喜欢她，因为她太傲了。
  That's hardly true.
  那不太可能是真的。

2. race away： 迅速跑掉（本课中的意思）；在赛马（或赛狗等）赌局中输掉
  He raced all his money away.
  他赌马把钱输了个精光。

3. sprint to the spot： 全速奔跑到出事地点
  He didn't have enough strength to sprint the last few metres.
  他没有足够的体力全速跑完最后的几米。
  Inspector Jack came to the spot where Mary was murdered.
  杰克警官来到玛丽被谋杀的地点。

4. the emergency room： 急诊室
  the dental department：牙科
  the surgical department：外科
  the X-ray department：放射科

5. take a brief look at somebody： 草草地给某人检查
  give somebody a thorough check：给某人做全面检查

6. be out of sight： 消失在视线之外
  Keep out of my sight.
  不要让我看见你。
  We came in sight of the shore at midday.
  中午时分，我们见到了海岸。

7. rush [run] all the way down： 一路冲[跑]下来
  I ran all the way to the office.
  我一路跑到办公室。

8. jump the queue： 不按次序排队，插队
  queue up：排队
  people in queues for buses：排队等公共汽车的人们

9. put several stitches to lips and gum： 在嘴唇及牙龈上缝几针
  I was about to put the girl to bed when I heard a knock at the door.
  我正要把小女孩放上床睡觉，忽然听见有人敲门。
  Let's put the resolution to the vote.
  咱们来投票表决吧。

10.The long and short of it was, ...
  简而言之，它的问题是……
  The long and (the) short of it is that he can't cope.
  总之，他这人拙于应付。

11. too silly for words： 无聊得无法用语言来表达
  What I couldn't explain in words, I did by signs.
  我不能用言语解释的东西，我就做手势。

12. all told： 总共（通常是总计以上提到的所有事物）
  ... All told, there are about 25 million Punjabi speakers in India.
  ……在印度大约总共有2500万人说旁遮普语。

13. leave the hospital alive： 活着离开医院
  In those days many people were buried alive.
  那时侯很多人被活埋了。

14. plunge somebody into a heavy debt： 使某人债台高筑
  The company is plunging deeper into debt.
  这家公司的债台正越筑越高。
  He plunged himself into grief.
  他陷入悲痛中。

15. clinical trial use： 临床试用
  a clinical diagnosis：临床诊断
  clinical medicine：临床医学
  clinical thermometer：体温表

16. apply for： 请求，申请
  She applied for membership in the Party.
  她申请入党。
  We applied to many medical institutions for assistance.
  我们向多家医疗机构求援。

17. get turned down： 被拒绝
  I have turned down his offer.
  我没有接受他提出的帮助。
  My proposal was turned down.
  我的建议没有被采纳。

18. be hard up： 经济困难，缺钱
  He said he was a bit hard up this month and asked me to lend him $50.
  他说他这个月手头有点儿紧，要我借给他50美元。

19. jump at the chance： 抓住这次机会
  He will probably jump at the invitation.
  他可能会立即接受这邀请的。
  She naturally jumped at the chance to come to China.
  她当然急忙抓住了这次访华机会。

20. be under no illusion about the predicament： 对困境有充分的估计
  I'm under no illusion as to what I meant to you. But you made an impression on my mind.
  我对你意味着什么，这一点我很清楚。但你却留在了我的脑海里。
  I have no illusions about his ability.
  我对他的能力不存幻想。

21. break the camel's back： 最后的致命一击
  源于the straw that broke the camel's back――压断骆驼脊梁的稻草（最后的致命一击）
  Losing my job was bad enough but ending the relationship like that was the straw that broke the camel's back.
  丢掉工作已经够糟了，现在又这样就结束了我们的关系，真是没法再忍受了。

22. beg for alms from all quarters： 四处乞求救济
  In those years she was forced to beg for a living.
  那些年她被迫出去讨饭。

23. meet with rebuffs： 遭到拒绝
  Again and again the test met with no success.
  这个试验一再遭到失败。
  We are bound to meet with all sorts of difficulties before final victory.
  在取得最后胜利之前，我们一定会遇到种种困难。

24. as a token of recognition for his brilliant work： 作为承认他出色的工作的一种表示
  voice recognition for many languages：兼容多种语言的语音识别
  He gave no sign of recognition of the old friends.
  他完全没有认出老朋友们的表示。
  Please accept it as a recognition of your services.
  作为酬劳请收下它。
