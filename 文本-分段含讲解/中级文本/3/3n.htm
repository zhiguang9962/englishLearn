1. queue for a bus： 排队等公共汽车
  We had to queue for hours to get in.
  我们必须排几个小时的长队才能进去。
  He asked me to queue up at the box office.
  他叫我在售票处排队。
  Queue here for a taxi.
  在此排队等候出租汽车。

2. a wad of pound notes： 一沓一镑的钞票
  She pulled a wad of $10 notes out of her purse.
  她从钱包里掏出一沓面额10美元的钞票。

3. make a fuss： 大惊小怪
  I hate it when you always make a great fuss about trifles.
  我讨厌你总是小题大做。
  Don't make so much fuss over the children.
  不要对孩子照顾得太过分。
  What a fuss about nothing!
  真是无事生非！

4. get people into trouble： 让别人倒霉
  Don't mention my name or you'll get me into trouble.
  别提我的名字，免得给我惹麻烦。
  He got into trouble with the police.
  他落到了警方手里。
  I'm sorry to have to put you to so much trouble.
  真抱歉，给您添了这么多麻烦。

5. the last words of the news： （一条）新闻的最后几句话
  I knew this was my last hope of winning.
  我知道这是我获胜的惟一希望了。
  You are the last person I want to see.
  你是我最不想见的人。
  I've said my last word ― take it or leave it.
  我的最终意见已经说过了――悉听尊便。

6. warn somebody (not) to do something： 警告，告诫某人（不要）做某事
  We warned him to stay away.
  我们警告他不要靠近。
  He warned her not to tell anyone else about it.
  他叮嘱她不要和别人讲这事。
  I was warned not to go there.
  曾有人告诫我不要去那里。
