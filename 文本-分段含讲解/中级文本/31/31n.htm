1. leave off： 不再穿（本课中的意思）；停止，中断
  It had stopped raining, so they left off their raincoats.
  雨停了，他们脱掉了雨衣。
  Today I left off work earlier than usual.
  今天我下班比平时早。

2. call on somebody： 拜访某人
  They had come half a dozen times to call on me.
  他们曾来看望过我五六次。

3. That was a treat.
  那可真难得。
  It's always such a treat when you stop by.
  你过访寒舍总是令人高兴不已。
  What a treat it is to work with you!
  和你一起工作可真是难得！

4. it goes without saying that ...： 更不用说……
  It goes without saying that he's a qualified teacher.
  毫无疑问，他是一个合格的教师。
  It goes without saying that work without rest will do harm to health.
  只工作，不休息，显然对健康有害。

5. be lined with： ……排列成行（本课中的意思）；以……为里衬
  The streets were lined with cars.
  街上汽车排列成行。
  Each case is lined with foam plastic in order to protect the goods against pressure.
  箱子里垫有泡沫塑料以免货物受压。

6. make the rounds of the temple statues： 参拜庙中的神像
  Make the Rounds of the Seven Gods of Good Luck.
  七福神巡游。
  The watchmen who make the rounds in the city found me.
  城中巡逻的看守遇见了我。

7. be rooted out： 根除掉
  More important, they should put aside their prejudices and selfish interests to root out terrorism collectively and relentlessly.
  更重要的是，他们要捐弃成见，抛开私利，结合集体正义力量，毫不手软地将恐怖主义斩草除根。
  Anarchism is deeply rooted in the country.
  在该国无政府主义根深蒂固。

8. in the open： 在露天；公开（本课中的意思）
  They often see the films in the open.
  他们经常看露天电影。
  The hearsay about a minister's resignation has now become a fact; everything is now in the open.
  有关部长辞职的传言如今已经成为事实，现在所有的事情都公开了。

9. in any case： 无论如何
  In any case, finish this work today.
  无论如何，今天完成这项工作。
  It may rain tomorrow, but they are going home in any case.
  明天可能下雨，但不管怎样，他们都打算回家去。

10. be deprived of： 被剥夺
  She was deprived of her sight by the accident.
  那次事故使她丧失了视力。
  He was deprived of schooling at ten.
  他10岁时就失学了。

11. show somebody the way into： 带某人进入
  If you wish to follow Jesus Christ, he will show you the way into the Kingdom of Heaven.
  你若希望跟随耶稣基督，他会带你进入天国。
  Please show me the way into your heart.
  请带我进入你的内心深处。

12. dress up： 盛装，打扮
  When I was a child I often enjoyed dressing up in my mother's clothes.
  我小时候常常喜欢穿着母亲的衣服玩。
  Jane dressed up in Elizabethan costume for the fancy-dress ball.
  简穿着伊丽莎白时代的服装去参加化装舞会。

13. come to an end： 结束，终止
  All good things must come to an end.
  一切好事总会结束的。（天下没有不散的宴席。好景无常。）
