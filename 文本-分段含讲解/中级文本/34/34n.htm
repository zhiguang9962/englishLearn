1. a farewell to unemployment： 告别失业（指找工作容易）
  a hearty handshake with affluence：和富裕亲切握手（指享有富裕生活）
  feel as happy as a lark and as proud as a peacock：感觉像百灵鸟一样快乐，像孔雀一样骄傲

2. turn up one's noses at： 对……嗤之以鼻
  They turned their noses up at the poor.
  他们瞧不起穷人。
  I offered him $30 a week but he turned his nose up at it.
  我提出给他周薪30美元，但他看不上。

3. get a good command of two foreign languages and Chinese： 精通两门外语和汉语
  As a Technical Account Manager, in addition to good communication skills, you must get a good command of both written and spoken English.
  作为技术支援经理，除了拥有较好的沟通技能，你还要精通英语写作和口语。

4. draw upon [on]： 利用，依靠
  I draw upon her strength a lot.
  我常常依靠她的支持。
  Much of his work drew on the folk music of Armenia.
  他的许多作品借用了亚美尼亚的民间音乐。

5. make a large purchase of： 大量购买……
  We spent some money for the purchase of the furniture necessary for our new house.
  我们花费了一些钱，购买新房子里必需的家具。

6. bring colossal losses to： 给……带来巨大损失
  His unfortunate death was a great loss to the firm.
  他的不幸去世对他的公司是个重大损失。

7. burn the candle at both ends: 过份地耗费精力，操劳过度
  You can't burn the candle at both ends forever.
  你不能永远这么过分地消耗精力.

8. Shame on those ...
  那些……真可耻!
  Baby, shame on you if you fool me once; shame on me if you fool me twice.
  宝贝，你愚弄我一次，是你可耻；你愚弄我两次，是我的羞耻！

9. be loaded with： 满载
  The lorry was loaded with bricks.
  卡车上载满了砖。
  They loaded us with gifts.
  他们给了我们很多礼物。

10. Last, but not least, ...
  最后，但同样重要的，……
  And last but not least there is the question of adequate funding.
  最后同样重要的是要有足够资金的问题。
  Last but not least, I must thank our host for his hospitality.
  最后但同样重要的一点是，我得感谢主人的热情好客。

11. open up new fields： 开辟新领域
  We decided to open up the island for cultivation.
  我们决定开发耕种这个岛。
  The October Revolution opened up a new epoch in world history.
  十月革命开辟了世界历史的新纪元。

12. serve one's country and one's people heart and soul: 全心全意为国家和人民服务
  I gave my heart and soul to you; now it's essential that you show a little love for me.
  我把自己的内心和灵魂都给了你，现在关键是你要对我表示一点爱意了。

13. My bet's on English.
  我的赌注放在英语上。
  He placed a 50-dollar bet on a horse in the first race.
  他在第一场比赛中，给一匹马下了50美元的赌注。
  My bet is that he won't agree.
  我敢打赌他是不会同意的。

14. communicate with： 通话，交流
  The Minister for Foreign Affairs has already communicated on this event with the American President.
  外交部长已经跟美国总统就此事件交换过意见了。
  Since then, she lost her ability to communicate with an audience.
  自那以后她再也无法引起观众的共鸣。
