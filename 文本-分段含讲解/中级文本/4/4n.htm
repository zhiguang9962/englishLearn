1. Pop Music： 流行音乐
  the top of the pops：最畅销的流行音乐唱片

2. in the centre of London： 伦敦市中心
  Beijing is the political, economic and cultural centre of China.
  北京是中国的政治、经济和文化中心。
  She likes to be the centre of attention.
  她喜欢成为人们注意的中心。

3. in the early hours of the morning： 凌晨

4. sit on the edge of his bed： 坐在他的床边上
  He fell off the edge of the cliff.
  他从悬崖边上摔了下来。
  Don't put your glasses on the edge of the desk.
  不要把眼镜放在桌边。
  They are on the edge of war.
  他们处于战争的边缘。

5. complain to somebody： 向某人抱怨
  The old woman is always complaining about something.
  那个老女人总是满腹牢骚。
  They complained to the manager that the wages were too low.
  他们向经理抱怨工资过低。

6. with great care： 小心翼翼地

7. a violent knocking on his door： 一阵猛烈的敲门声
  a violent storm：猛烈的风暴
  violent language：激烈的语言
  violent means：暴力手段

8. Of all the men who ever liked fresh air, not one liked it more than James Wilson.
  在所有酷爱新鲜空气的人当中，谁也赶不上詹姆斯・威尔逊。
  Nobody understands the situation better than you.
  谁也不如你了解情况。
  In the world, no one loves you more than I do.
  世界上没有人比我更爱你。

9. He always slept with his windows wide open.
  他睡觉时窗户总是敞开着。
  She sleeps with the light on.
  她开着灯睡觉。
  He has to walk with a stick in his hand.
  他得拄着拐棍走路。

10. an open car with no roof： 没有顶篷的汽车

11. in a manner： 以一种态度、方式
  She objected in a forceful manner.
  她表示坚决反对。
  I dislike her manner ― she's very hostile.
  我不喜欢她的态度――待人如仇敌。
  He had an absent-minded manner.
  他表现出心不在焉的样子。

12. on board： 在船上（也可指在飞机或车上）
  All on board!
  请大家上车！

13. on business： 因公，有事
  He was out of business.
  他破产了。
  Business is business.
  公事公办。
  That's no business of yours.
  不关你事。

14. keep the icy air out： 阻隔冷空气
  The trees keep out the wind.
  这些树可以挡风。
  He was so poor that he couldn't afford a jacket to keep out the cold.
  他穷得连一件御寒的夹克都买不起。

15. turn over in bed： 在床上翻身
  To whom should I turn over the key?
  我该把钥匙交给谁？
  The bike hit a rock and turned over.
  自行车撞到了石头上，翻了。
  She turned over a page or two, but was not interested.
  她翻看了一两页，但不感兴趣。

16. An idea came into his worried mind.
  闷闷不乐的他冒出一个念头。
  He turned the new idea over in his mind.
  他把这个新想法仔细考虑了一下。
  She has no idea how to manage people.
  她根本不知道如何做人事工作。

17. The glass was all in one piece.
  玻璃仍完好无损。
  They were lucky to get back in one piece.
  他们安全返回，十分幸运。
