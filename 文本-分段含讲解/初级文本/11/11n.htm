1. a visit to Conway Castle： 康威城堡之旅
  It was his first visit to his wife's parents.
  那是他第一次去看望岳父岳母。
  I am going on a visit to the seaside.
  我打算去海滨游玩。

2. get on a bus： 上公共汽车
  get off a bus：下公共汽车

3. Has anybody got any questions?
  还有谁有什么问题吗？
  I have got lunch in town.
  我已经在城里吃过午饭。
  You can get your information out of an encyclopedia.
  你可以从百科全书中查到资料。

4. get into： 进入
  As soon as he got into the car, it started moving.
  他一上车，车就开始起动了。
  Tom got into bed quickly when he heard his mother coming back home.
  汤姆听到妈妈回家的声音后，赶忙上床睡觉。

5. like something / somebody： 喜欢某物 / 某人
  I like apples.
  我喜欢吃苹果。
  How do you like this book?
  你觉得这本书怎么样？

6. not very much： 不太……
  口语中经常会碰到“not + 程度副词”的用法。
  "Thanks for your trouble." "Not at all, I enjoyed it."
  “给您添麻烦了。”“不必客气，我乐意为你帮忙。”
  There is not a little water in the glass.
  杯子里有很多水。

7. too： adv. 太，过分
  Mrs. Black worries too much.
  布莱克太太过分担心了。
  Tom drives too fast.
  汤姆开车太快了。

8. leave school： 毕业离校
  注意正确理解 school 前无冠词 the 时的语意。
  There will be no school tomorrow.
  明日学校放假。
  What do you plan to do when you finish school?
  你完成在校学业后打算干什么?

9. start work： 开始工作
  It is hard for him to start a business.
  对他来说，创业是艰难的。
  If you are ready, you may start your work.
  如果你准备好了，你可以开始工作了。

10.at the end of the term： 学期末
  He is down at the end of the garden.
  他在花园的尽头的那边。
  At the beginning of the year, he went to Australia.
  年初，他去了澳大利亚。

11. veterinary surgeon： （外科）兽医

12. Good gracious!
  天啊!（表示惊讶）
  类似的惊叹句有：My God!
  Good Heavens! / Great Heavens! / Gracious Heavens!

13. take care of： 照顾
  注意 take care of 与 take care 在语意上的区别：后者是“当心”的意思。
  Take care of yourself.
  照顾好你自己。
  Her job is to take care of the children.
  她的工作是照顾小孩。
  Take care that no mistakes be made.
  当心不要出错。

14. once： adv. 曾经，从前
  I once knew him.
  我以前认识他。
  It once seemed inconceivable to everyone that men should travel to the moon.
  人们一度认为，人飞上月球似乎是不可想象的。
