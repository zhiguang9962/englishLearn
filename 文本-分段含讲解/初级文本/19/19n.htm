1. the only son： 独生子
  The facts were known only to us.
  这些事只有我们才知道。
  I am the only girl in my family.
  我是家里唯一的女孩。

2. early the next day： 翌日一清早。
  The next morning, he found that he was in a strange place.
  第二天早晨，他发现自己在一个陌生的地方。

3. at the time： 当时，在那个时候
  I agreed at the time but later changed my mind.
  我当时同意了，但后来又改变了主意。
  I was living in Shanghai at the time.
  我那阵儿住在上海。

4. possess (= have / own)： vt. 拥有
  He possesses a house and two cars.
  他有一栋房子和两辆汽车。
  She possessed great tact.
  她具有非凡的智谋

5. get to school： 上学
  I broke my leg and finally got to work at home.
  我腿摔断了，最后只能在家工作。

6. This was a good opportunity to teach him a lesson.
  这是一次教育他的好机会。
  It is a great opportunity for you to be promoted.
  这是你升职的好机会。
  We should grasp every opportunity to success.
  我们应该抓住每个成功的机会。
  It is a lesson to me.
  这对我是一个教训。
  The teacher taught Jack a lesson for his bad behavior.
  老师因为杰克的恶劣表现批评了他一通。

7. How hard life was for the less fortunate people of the world.
  世界上穷苦人的生活是多么的艰苦。
  How nice of you!
  你真好！
  How beautiful she is in the dress!
  她穿上这件裙子多么漂亮啊！

8. in the same way as every other child goes： 跟其他所有孩子一样
  I went out the same way (that) I'd got in.
  我顺着进来的原路出去了。
  You needn't do anything in the same way as others do. Have it your own way.
  你没必要做每件事都和其他人一样。照你的方式做吧。

9. What's going on here? (= What's happening here?)
  这儿发生什么事了？
  The machine has gone on the fritz.
  机器出了毛病。
  Where did it happen?
  在哪儿发生的？

10. leave by the front door： 从前门离开
  Please enter by the right door.
  请走右门。
  I go by the house every day.
  我每天路过那所房子。

11. in a hurry： 匆忙
  You always seem to be in a hurry.
  你似乎总是很匆忙。
  In her hurry she forgot to bring money with her.
  在匆忙中，她忘了带钱。

12. come on： 快点
  come on 用于祈使句时通常是鼓励某人做某事，尤其是促其加速、努力或试一试。
  Come on, we'll be late for the school.
  快点，我们上学要迟到了。
  Come on, you will make it.
  试试吧，你会成功的。

13. waste time： 浪费时间
  I regretted wasting a lot of money on such unuseful things.
  我后悔在这些没用的东西上浪费了很多钱。
  It is a pity to waste an opportunity for going abroad.
  浪费了一个出国机会，真可惜。
  Don't waste the food; there isn't much.
  不要浪费食物，没有多少了。

14. just a minute (= just a moment)： 等一下
  I have seen him just now.
  我刚才见过他。
  She has gone to America just after he married with Mary.
  他和玛丽结婚后，她就去美国了。

15. I don't think you are telling me the truth.
  我想你没有说实话。
  注意 I don't think ... 的译法是将否定放在从句里，这是中英文说话的一种区别。
  I don't think they will win the prize.
  我想他们不会赢得奖品。
  I don't think you are right. (= I think you are wrong.)
  我认为你错了。

16. in jail for house-breaking： 因溜门撬锁入狱
  He was sentenced to jail for six months.
  他被判处入狱6个月。
  He went to jail for killing.
  他因杀人罪入狱。
