1. have a holiday： 度假，休假
  Jim and I are going to have a holiday.
  我和吉姆准备去度假。
  I've already had my holidays this year.
  我今年已经度过假了。

2. go to evening classes： 上夜校
  I am going to evening classes for my English.
  我打算去上夜校补习英语。
  Jane went to evening classes for drive.
  简上夜校学习开车。

3. have Chinese lessons： 上汉语课
  How many lessons do you have every day?
  你们每天上多少节课？

4. study hard： 努力学习
  注意副词 hard 的使用。
  Study hard, or you will get behind others.
  努力学习，否则你就会落后于其他人。
  Our victory was hard won.
  我们的胜利来之不易。

5. a few weeks later： 几个星期以后
  But some time later it began to snow.
  但过了些时候天开始下雪了。
  Many years later, in Washington, D.C., he spoke to a crowd of 250,000 people.
  许多年以后，在华盛顿特区，他向二十五万美国人民发表了演说。

6. make somebody do something： 让某人做某事
  注意 make 的动词不定式不带 to。
  Father made me do homework when everybody else was having a good time.
  别人玩的时候爸爸让我写作业。
  Mary made me clean the room for her.
  玛丽让我给她打扫房间。

7. dress up： 打扮一下，化装
  She likes to dress up for a party.
  她喜欢把自己打扮得漂漂亮亮去参加晚会。
  They were dressed up in Victorian clothes.
  他们化装成维多利亚时代的人。

8. Many happy returns of the day.
  长命百岁。（祝福用语）
  Wish you all the best!
  祝你一路顺风！
 
9. Here's a present for you.
  给你一份礼物。
  Here's the money.
  给你钱。
  "Give me a glass of water, please." "Here you are."
  “请给我一杯水。”“给你。”

10. Would you like to ...?
  你想……吗？
  此句型常用于邀请或建议。
  Would you like to see a film with me tonight?
  今晚你想和我一起看电影吗？
  Would you like to have another cake?
  你想再来一块蛋糕吗？
