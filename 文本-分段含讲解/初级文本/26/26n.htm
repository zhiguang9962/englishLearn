1. spend their summer holidays in England： 在英国度暑假
  I am going to spend my holidays abroad this year.
  我今年准备到国外去度假。
  How do you spend your leisure?
  你是怎样消磨空闲时间的？

2. in the past： 过去
  I often met her in the past, but now seldom.
  过去我经常碰到她，但现在很少了。
  How did you lead your life in years past?
  在过去的几年中你是怎样生活的？

3. at the seaside (= by the sea)： 在海边
  We are going to the seaside for our holiday.
  我们打算去海滨度假。

4. however： adv. 然而，可是
  The book is expensive; however, it's worth it.
  这本书很贵，却很值。
  He hasn't arrived. He may, however, come later.
  他还未到，不过，他可能过一会儿来。

5. make a lot of money in his business： 做生意挣了许多钱
  He made a profit of $1000.
  他赚了1000美元。
  You only consider how to make money without taking care of your own health.
  你只顾着如何赚钱，却毫不关心你自己的身体状况。

6. go around： 四处游逛
  She went around and tried to think out a good idea.
  她走来走去，试图想出一个好主意。
  I looked around but found nobody.
  我往四周看看，但一个人都没有发现。
  They walked around the town.
  他们在城里四处走动。

7. see the sights of that famous city： 观看那座著名城市的风光
  The Grand Canyon is one of the sights of the world.
  （科罗拉多）大峡谷是世界名胜之一。

8. They expected that they would have to go to bed hungry.
  他们以为只能饿着肚子去睡觉。
  注意 hungry 在句中的用法。它本身是形容词，虽然放在动词短语 go to bed 的后面，但实际上
  是对主语 they 的补充说明。另外，expect 在这里当“料想、认为”讲，一般用于口语表达。
  I expected he would come, but in fact he broke his promise.
  我以为他会来，但事实是他失言了。

9. arrive at： 到达
  When did you arrive at the station?
  你是几点钟到车站的？
  Good luck has arrived at last.
  好运气终于来了。

10. be used to (doing) something： 习惯于，适应
  I was used to getting up at six o'clock.
  我过去习惯六点起床。
  You will soon become used to the cold weather here.
  你很快就会适应这里的寒冷天气。

11. serve dinner： 招待晚餐
  He was so kind to serve me a wonderful dinner.
  他非常友善，以盛宴款待我。
  Would you please serve coffee hot?
  麻烦你把咖啡趁热端上来。

12. hardly： adv. 几乎不，刚刚
  This is hardly the time to discuss such matters.
  现在讨论这事根本不合适。
  She is hardly ever late.
  她几乎从不迟到。

13. leave time for somebody to do something： 给某人留时间做某事
  I must leave myself plenty of time, or there will be something wrong with my work.
  我必须给自己留出充裕的时间，否则我的工作会出错的。
  How much time are you going to leave for me to do it?
  你打算给我留出多长时间做这件事情？

14. go out walking： 出去散步
  walking 作 go out 的伴随状语。
  sit around talking：围坐一起聊天
  He sat there watching, but said nothing.
  他坐在那里看着, 但没说什么。

15. be ahead of： 在……的前面
  That company is ahead of other makers of spare parts for the computer.
  那家公司制造计算机零部件比别家的业绩好。
  Our company is ahead in developing the new technology.
  我们公司在发展新科技方面处于领先地位。

16. It was 7:30 when Bill and Lise came in.
  比尔和丽兹进门的时候是七点半。（强调句）
  It was on Friday that all the snow fell.
  是星期五那天下雪的。
  It was Tom who had helped me out of the trouble.
  是汤姆曾经帮助我摆脱了麻烦。

17. for ages： 好久，很长一段时间
  He left China for America ages ago.
  在许多年前，他离开了中国，前往美国。
  I haven't seen him for ages.
  我有很长时间没有看见他了。

18. a letter of complaint： 投诉信
  a registered letter：挂号信
  a letter of application：申请书
  a letter of appointment：委任书
