1. find something wrong with： 发现什么不对劲
  Did I do anything wrong?
  我做错什么了？
  I'm sorry. I got it wrong.
  对不起，我弄错了。

2. send the results of the tests direct to Mrs. Black's doctor：
  直接把化验结果寄给了布莱克夫人的医生
  After I walked out of the theatre, I went home direct.
  我走出剧院后，直接回家了。
  The next flight doesn't go to Hainan direct, it goes by way of Guangzhou.
  下一次航班不直接飞往海南，要经过广州。

3. a list of： 一列，一栏
  I must make a list of things to buy.
  我得列个购物单。

4. the cause of： ……的原因
  Boldness is the cause of his failure.
  鲁莽是他失败的原因。
  The heavy rain was the cause of the flood.
  大雨是导致发生洪水的原因。

5. find something doing something： 发现某人正在做某事
  When I woke up, I found him writing a letter.
  我醒来的时候，发现他正在写信。
  I found a thief trying to break into the office when I arrived.
  我到的时候发现有个小偷企图闯入办公室。

6. a big basket full of packages： 装满大包小包的一个大篮子
  a pair of shoes covered with dirt：布满尘土的一双鞋
  two eyes full of tears：充满泪水的两只眼睛

7. in surprise： 惊讶地
  I looked at him in surprise ― I didn't expect to see him here.
  我惊奇地看着他――没想到在这儿会见到他。
  It's a great surprise for me to receive his gift.
  对于我来说，收到他的礼物是个巨大的惊喜。

8. allow somebody to do something： 允许某人做某事
  Allow me to introduce myself.
  请允许我自我介绍。
  We don't allow such things to be done.
  我们不容许有这种事情发生。

9. What was the party like last night?
  昨晚的晚会如何？
  What is she like?
  她长得什么样？
  What will the result be like?
  结果会如何？

10. What a shame! (= What a pity!)
  真可惜！
  It is a shame to waste so much food.
  这样浪费食物太可惜了。
  Tom hung his head in shame.
  汤姆因害羞而低下了头。

11. take a sleeping pill： 服用一粒安眠药
  Don't forget to take your medicine on time.
  不要忘了按时吃药。

12. used to do something： 过去常常做某事
  I used to go to visit him.
  我以前常去看他。
  Used he to come by bus? / Did he use to come by bus?
  他过去常乘公共汽车来吗？

13. work overtime： 工作加班加点（overtime 作副词）
  be on overtime：在加班（overtime 作名词）
  overtime pay：加班费（overtime 作形容词）

14. under a terrible strain： 过度疲劳，极度紧张
  Working overtime made her under a terrible strain.
  加班加点的工作令她处于极度紧张状态。
  The strain of sleepless nights made her ill.
  她由于连夜不眠的过度劳累而病了。
  I have been suffering from the strain of modern life.
  我一直苦于现代生活的紧张。

15. anyway： adv. 总之
  I don't know if it was lost or stolen; anyway, I can't find it.
  我不知道它是丢了还是被偷了；不管怎么说，我找不着它了。
  Anyway, only if you like, all is OK.
  总之，只要你喜欢，一切就OK了。

16. instead： adv. 代替
  Because of the traffic jam we had to walk instead.
  由于塞车，我们不得不改为步行。
  If you cannot go, let me go instead.
  如果你不能去，让我替你去。
