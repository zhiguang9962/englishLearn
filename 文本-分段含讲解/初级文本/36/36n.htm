1. on the edge of a forest： 在森林边上
  I live in a house on the edge of town.
  我住在城镇边的一间房子里。
  He was on the edge of bankruptcy because of an unwise decision-making.
  由于一个不明智的决策，他处于破产的边缘。

2. set out： 出发
  I set out at dawn.
  我天一亮就出发了。
  We set out on the last stage of our journey.
  我们开始了旅行的最后一程。

3. plenty of： 许多
  plenty of 既可修饰可数名词，也可修饰不可数名词。
  There are plenty of men out of work.
  有很多人失业。
  We have plenty of time to catch the train.
  我们有足够的时间赶上火车。

4. make sure： 确定
  Can you make sure he will come?
  你能确定他会来吗？
  I think the door's locked, but I'd better go and make sure.
  我想门已经锁了，不过我最好还是去查看一下。

5. be about to do something： 正打算做某事
  I was about to leave when my secretary called me back.
  我快要离开时，我的秘书叫住了我。
  I am not about to agree with you.
  我一点也不认同你的观点。

6. cry out： 大声呼喊
  speak out：大声说出
  shout out：大声叫喊
  call out：大声喊叫

7. grant you and your wife three wishes： 满足你和你妻子的三个愿望
  They granted me permission to go there.
  他们让我去那儿。
  I was granted a pension.
  我得到了养老金。

8. at least： 至少
  "I waited at least an hour," I said angrily.
  “我等了至少一个小时，”我生气地说道。
  "How much time do you need to finish your work?" "At least three days."
  “你需要多长时间完成你的工作？”“至少三天。”

9. right now (= at once)： 马上
  Clean the classroom right now; the schoolmaster is coming.
  马上打扫教室，校长来了。
  Get out right now!
  马上滚出去！

10. I wish that ...： 我希望……（虚拟语气）
  I wished I hadn't done it.
  我想要是我没这样做该多好。
  I wish I were a bird with a pair of free limbs.
  但愿我能变成一只鸟，长着一对自由的翅膀。

11. The only thing to do was to wish it on the table again, which the woodman did.
  惟一能做的就是愿这香肠回到桌子上去，樵夫也的确这样做了。
  which 是一关系代词，引导一个非限制性定语从句，代替它前面整个句子。
  I said nothing, which made him still more angry.
  我一声不响, 这就使他更加生气。
  He changed his mind again, which I had got used to.
  他又改变了主意，我已经习惯他这样了。

12. threaten somebody with a knife： 拿着刀恐吓某人
  I was threatened with a beating if I didn't obey him.
  如果我不服从，他扬言要打我。
  He threatened the congressman with public exposure.
  他威胁说要向公众揭发这个国会议员。

13. get a good look at somebody： 好好看某人一眼
  When he left he gave her a mournful look.
  他离开的时候悲伤地看了她一眼。
  May I have a look at it please?
  请让我看一看好吗？

14. do much damage to the flat： 对公寓做太大破坏
  The storm did a lot of damage to the crops.
  暴风雨使庄稼受到了很大损失。
  The earthquake did great damage to them.
  地震给他们造成了巨大损失。
