1. be well known： 众所周知
  He was well known here for his warm heart to help others.
  因为热心肠，喜欢帮助别人，这里很多人都知道他。
  It is well known that he is a foolish guy.
  众所周知，他是个愚蠢的家伙。

2. social blunders： 社交中的错误
  He made a terrible blunder because of carelessness.
  因为疏忽，他铸成大错。
  You've made an awful blunder.
  你做了一件大错特错的事。

3. expand his circle of friends： 扩大他的朋友圈子。
  I decided to expand my short novel into a long one.
  我决定把我的短篇小说扩展为长篇。
  The company has expanded from having one office to having twelve.
  这个公司已从拥有一个办事处发展到拥有十二个办事处了。

4. an invitation to a fashionable banquet： 上流社会的宴会请柬
  We had only an invitation to parties.
  我们只有一张参加晚会的请柬。
  I attended their wedding banquet last Sunday.
  我上星期日参加了他们的婚宴。

5. grow one's reputation： 提升威望
  That made him lose his reputation.
  那件事令他名誉扫地。
  He is a man of good reputation.
  他是个名誉很好的人。

6. whether ... or ...： 无论……或是……
  It's up to you, whether to go or not.
  无论走不走，由你决定。
  I shall go, whether you come with me or stay at home.
  不论你来还是留在家中，我都要去。

7. even though： 即使，尽管
  I will go there even though you don't accompany me.
  即使你不陪我，我也要去那里。
  I like her even though she can be nagging.
  尽管她可能会比较唠叨，但我还是喜欢她。

8. between the first and second course： 在第一道和第二道菜之间
  I will come back between 11 o'clock and 12 o'clock.
  我会在11点到12点之间回来。
  Between astonishment and joy, I could hardly say a word.
  我惊喜交加，一句话也说不出来。
  We ordered three courses：soup, meat and vegetables, and rice.
  我们点了三道菜：汤、肉和蔬菜，还有米饭。

9. the Secretary of the Interior： 内政部长
  the Secretary of State：[美]国务卿；[英]国务大臣
  the Minister of Education：教育部长
  the Prime Minister：首相，总理
  the Minister for Foreign Affairs：外交部长

10. in spite of： 不顾
  She went out to date with him in spite of her mother's disagreement.
  尽管她妈妈不同意，她还是出去和他约会了。
  In spite of great efforts he failed to carry his plans through.
  尽管他作出了巨大努力，他还是没能完成计划。

11. matter： vi. 有关系，要紧
  It doesn't matter if you are late.
  你如果迟到也没关系。
  It doesn't matter about the price.
  价钱不是问题。

12. He was the man for the job.
  他就是这个职位的合适人选。
  Do you find the right person for the vacancy?
  你找到这个空缺的合适人选了吗？

13. Thank goodness!
  谢天谢地！
  = Thank God!
  God be thanked!

14. Hands up!
  举起手来！
  hand：n. 手；v. 传递，交给
  Please put up your hand if you can answer this question.
  如果你会回答这个问题，请举手。
  Please hand me that pencil.
  请把那支铅笔递给我。
  You must hand in your homework by the end of this week.
  你必须在这周末之前把作业交上来。

15. far more expensive than ...： 比……贵得多
  far 在这里作副词，用于修饰比较级，意为“更……”，起到强调作用。
  She is far better than me at painting.
  她画画比我好得多。
  I am so sorry. It is far beyond my powers.
  我很抱歉，这远非我力所能及。
  He always studies far into the night
  他总是学习到深夜。

16. What in the world ...： 到底……
  这个句子不完整，可以是 “What in the world are you?” 或是 “What in the world is
  going on?”。 in the world 意思等同于 on earth，用于加强语气。
  Where did he go in the world?
  他到底去哪儿了
  I don't know what on earth she is going to do.
  我不知道她究竟想怎样。
