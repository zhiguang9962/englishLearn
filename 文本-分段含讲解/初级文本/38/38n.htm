1. at one time： 曾经
  He drank heavily at one time.
  他曾经一度酗酒。
  I worked as a secretary at one time.
  我曾经做过秘书。

2. in his younger days： 在他年轻的时候
  In those days we lived our life hardly.
  那个时候我们生活很艰苦。
  He did many silly things in his younger days.
  他年轻的时候做过许多傻事。

3. have a habit of (doing) something： 有做某事的习惯
  Mary has a habit of smoking a cigarette after dinner.
  玛丽有晚饭后抽一根烟的习惯。
  His father has a habit of getting up early.
  他父亲有早起的习惯。

4. interrupt a letter with a few words on other subjects：
  在一封信中插进几句关于其它问题的话
  Don't interrupt me when I am on the phone.
  我打电话的时候不要打断我。
  What did he say on the subject of money?
  在钱的问题上他是怎么说的？

5. have (no) connection with ...： 与……有 / 无关联
  We have connections with various international corporations in Europe.
  我们同欧洲的多家跨国公司有业务往来。
  Is there a connection between her death and him?
  她的死和他有关联吗？

6. deal with： 处理，安排
  The teacher deals fairly with his pupils.
  这个教师公平地对待他的学生。
  She is easy to deal with.
  她很容易打交道。

7. be willing to do something： 愿意做某事
  I am willing to do anything for you.
  我愿意为你做任何事情。
  I'm willing to concede that I have hurt you, but that's not my real intention.
  我可以承认我是伤害了你，但那并不是我的本意。

8. work beyond office hours (work overtime)： 加班
  She did not get home until beyond midnight.
  直到午夜后她才回家。
  It's quite beyond me why she married such an old man who she didn't love at all.
  我实在无法理解为什么她会嫁给这样一个她根本不爱的老头。

9. feel rather sorry for oneself： 垂头丧气，灰心失望
  I felt rather sorry for myself because I lost my wallet.
  我垂头丧气，因为丢了钱包。
  I feel sorry for him.
  我为他感到惋惜。

10. ring up： 打电话
  Ring (up) the airport and find out when the plane leaves.
  给机场打个电话，问清楚什么时候起飞。
  I'll ring you as soon as I arrive there.
  我一到那里就给你打电话。

11. urge somebody to do something： 催促，怂恿某人做某事
  He urged me to study.
  他催促我学习。
  I urged her to reconsider this matter, but she persisted on her original decision.
  我竭力怂恿她重新考虑一下这件事情，但她坚持原来的决定。

12. be sure about somebody / something： 对某人 / 某事有把握
  Do you feel sure about it?
  你能对这有把握吗？
  I'm not sure about the practicality of your plan.
  我还无法确定你们的那个计划的可行性如何。

13. explain something to somebody： 向某人解释某事
  Would you please explain your reason for being late to us?
  请你给我们解释一下你迟到的理由好吗？
  I explained my plan to the manager.
  我把自己的方案向经理做了说明。

14. persuade somebody to do something： 劝说某人做某事
  He persuaded me to go there with him.
  他说服我和他一起去那里。
  I was persuaded to agree with him.
  我被劝服同意他的看法。

15. keep something / somebody away： 让某物 / 某人离远点
  Keep away from the scene of the accident.
  勿靠近事故现场。
  You'd better keep him away.
  你最好让他离远点 。

16. take it as a favour： 为之荣幸
  I'll take it as a favour if you would go to the concert with me.
  如果您愿意和我一起去听音乐会，我将为之荣幸。
  I shall esteem it a favour if you would spare your time to visit my new house.
  如果您愿意抽时间光临我的新居，我将不胜荣幸。

17. happen to something / somebody： 发生在……身上（常指不幸的事情）
  What will happen to the children if they break up?
  如果他们离婚孩子们将怎么办？

18. a quiz on general knowledge： 常识测验
  I was invited to take part in a quiz on music.
  我被邀请参加一个有关音乐的竞赛。

19. In a way that's right.
  在某种程度上，那是对的。
  You could not be right in any way.
  在任何情况下，你都不可能是对的。
  In some ways you are right.
  在某些方面你是对的。

20. It's on the tip of my tongue.
  就在我嘴边上（记得某件事，但一时说不出来）。
  Her name is on the tip of my tongue, but I just can't think of it.
  她的名字就在嘴边上，可我就是想不起来了。
