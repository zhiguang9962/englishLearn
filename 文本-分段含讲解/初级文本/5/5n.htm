1. talk about： 谈论，提及
  Finally the president of the meeting talked about the pros and cons of the issue.
  最后大会主席谈论了这一议题中的正反两方。
  He talks about London in this book.
  他在这本书中谈到了对伦敦的看法。
  Are you talking about last week?
  你是在说上个星期的事吗？

2. their plans for the summer： 他们的暑期计划
  Do you have any plans for the evening?
  你晚上有什么计划吗？
  We made a five-year plan for expanding the national economy.
  我们为发展国民经济制定了一个五年计划。

3. go to work： 上班，工作
  Let's go to school.
  我们去上学吧。
  Would you like to go to cinema with me?
  你想和我去看电影吗？

4. go to camp： 去宿营
  The herdsmen camped themselves on the grasslands.
  牧民们在草原上扎营住宿。
  We pitched our camp by a lake.
  我们在湖边扎营。

5. stay in the city： 待在城里
  Did you stay at home yesterday?
  你昨天是待在家里吗？
  Why did you stay away from school?
  你为什么不上学？

6. all day： 整天。
  I don't know what you are doing all day.
  我不知道你整日都在做什么。
  He has worked hard all year.
  他全年工作都努力。

7. work with ...： 与……合作；对……起作用
  It's my pleasure to work with you.
  与你共事我感到很高兴。
  It seems that the sleeping pills don't work with her.
  安眠药似乎对她不起作用。

8. on weekends： 周末。
  I don't work at the weekend.
  我周末不工作。
  The library is closed on weekends.
  图书馆周末不开放。

9. go to the beach： 去海滩
  与 go to the mountains 一样，表示某项旅游活动。
  I like sunbathing on the beach.
  我喜欢在沙滩上晒日光浴。
  She used to take a walk along the beach.
  她过去常常沿海滨散步。

10. go swimming and boating： 去游泳，去划船
  由 go 后跟动名词构成，类似的短语还有 go skating，go shopping，go shooting，
  go camping 等。
  We used to go camping every summer when we were students.
  当学生的时候我们每年夏天都去野营。

11. play basketball： 打篮球
  注意 play 作为及物动词后跟 basketball，football，tennis，chess 等球类或棋类名词，表示
  进行某项体育运动时，play 和这些名词之间无须加冠词 the；但 play 后跟某种乐器作“演奏”讲
  时，play 和乐器名词之间要用冠词 the。另外，play 也可用于不及物动词，通过使用介词再后跟
  上述两类名词。
  Can you play on the piano?
  你会弹钢琴吗？
  They are playing at chess.
  他们在下棋。

12. sit around a campfire： 坐在篝火周围
  sit around 还可指“闲坐着”，什么事都不愿做或不能做。
  I've been sitting around waiting for her phone all day.
  我一整天都坐在那儿等着她来电话。

13. at night： 夜里。
  Yesterday he worked far into the night.
  昨天他工作至深夜。
  I arrived at the hotel late at night.
  我深夜到达了旅馆。

14. sound wonderful： 听起来真美妙
  His argument sounds reasonable.
  他的观点听起来有道理。
  Your idea sounds a good one.
  你的想法听起来很好。

15. so long： 再见
  表达“再见”的短语除了good-bye / bye-bye 还有 see you later，
  farewell（常含有永别或不容易再见面的意思）。

16. have fun： 玩得开心
  等同于 have a good time。
  He said it only for fun; you needn't be too serious.
  他是说着玩的，你无须太认真。
  There's no fun in spending the whole afternoon talking with him.
  和他谈了一下午没有意思。

17. give my regards to somebody： 代我向某人问好
  Give the family my best regards.
  祝您全家幸福。

18. beautifully： adv. 美好地，漂亮地
  beautifully 除了指穿衣打扮漂亮外，也常用来说活干得漂亮。
  She danced beautifully.
  她跳舞跳得很优美。
  China's pottery is beautifully finished and has enjoyed a high prestige in the world.
  中国的陶器加工精美，享誉世界。

19. dry： adj. 干的；vt. 弄干
  My mouth is dry.
  我口渴了。
  I dried my hair in the sun.
  我在太阳底下把头发晒干。

20. put away： 放好，收起来；存钱
  Put your toys away in the cupboard, when you've finished playing.
  不玩儿了，你就把玩具放进柜子里去。
  He has no children, so he has to get some money put away for his retirement.
  他没有子女，所以必须存点钱以备退休之用。
