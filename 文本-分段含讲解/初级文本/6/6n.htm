1. in the country： 在乡村
  Do you like the country music?
  你喜欢乡村音乐吗？
  Which do you prefer to live in a city or in the country?
  你喜欢住在城里还是住在乡下？

2. stay with： 和……待在一起
  stay with 后接人时指“和某人待在一起”，后接物时往往表示“把某事坚持或继续下去”。
  I stayed the week with my parents.
  我那星期和父母待在一起。
  I decided to stay with my original plan.
  我决定坚持我原来的计划。

3. go for a drive in the car： 开车去兜风
  It is a long drive to the village.
  开车到那个村庄需要很长时间。
  Do you have a drive's licence?
  你有驾驶执照吗？

4. Where did you meet them all?
  你是在哪儿碰见他们所有人的？
  Mom said that Dad would take us all to the park tomorrow.
  妈妈说爸爸明天会带我们所有人去公园。
  Henry is still doing his assignments. He can't watch TV until he has finished them all.
  亨利还在做他的作业。他必须全做完了才能看电视。

5. wave to somebody： 向某人挥手
  He waved excitedly to his companion.
  他兴奋地向他的伙伴挥了挥手。
  They waved at us from across the room.
  他们从房间那一头向我们招手。

6. be pleased： 感到高兴，满意
  We are very pleased with her decision.
  我们对她的决定很满意。
  He was pleased with himself.
  他洋洋自得。

7. with a happier heart： 更高兴
  He does everything with all his heart.
  他做每件事情都全心全意。
  She went back home with a heavy heart.
  她心情沉重地回到了家。

8. say to oneself： 自言自语，心里想
  What did you say to yourself？
  你自言自语什么呢？

9. have something to think about： 有心事
  Is there anything else to think about?
  还有别的事要考虑吗？
  I think she must have something to do with this matter.
  我想她一定和这件事有关。

10. during the rest of his journey： 在他后面的旅途中
  Many people suffered food shortages during the war.
  许多人在战争期间受食品短缺之苦。
  She had been staying in Taiwan for the rest of her life.
  她余生一直待在台湾。

11. make somebody happy： 让某人愉快
  His words made me very angry.
  他的话令我很生气。
  Have I made myself clear?
  我讲清楚了没有？

12. an ice cream parlor： 冷饮店
  a beauty parlor：美容院
  a funeral parlor：殡仪馆

13. order their favorite desserts： 要了她们最喜欢吃的甜食
  Would you like to order another hamburger?
  你要不要再来个汉堡？
  Bananas are my favorite fruit.
  香蕉是我最喜爱的水果。
  Do you like the frozen dessert?
  你喜欢吃冷冻甜食吗？

14. look at / in the newspaper： 看报纸
  a daily newspaper：日报
  a weekly newspaper：周刊，周报
  a newspaper office：报社
  a newspaper report：新闻报道

15. find out： 找出，查明
  Please find out what time Mr. Black will come.
  请查一查布莱克先生什么时候来。
  He decided to find out who did that.
  他决定查明那是谁做的。

16. on page nine： 在第九版
  Many women also read the sports pages and the financial pages.
  许多女人也读体育版和财经版。
  Please turn the book to page sixteen.
  请把书翻到16页。
