1. a heavy snowstorm： 一场很大的暴风雪
  a heavy rain：一场大雨
  a heavy punch：重重一击
  a heavy drinker：一个酒瘾极大的人

2. be full of： 充满
  I woke up in the middle of the night and found the room was full of smoke.
  我半夜醒来，发现屋里全是烟。
  The textbook is full of new words.
  这本教科书里面都是新单词。

3. pay somebody to do something： 花钱雇人做某事
  He was paid to teach in home.
  有人花钱请他做家教。
  I paid three dollars for a hamburger.
  我花三美元买了一个汉堡包。
  He paid me on an hourly wage.
  他按小时给我支付工资。

4. the path from his garage to his gate： 从车库到大门之间的小道
  There was a narrow path through the forest.
  有一条窄道穿过森林。
  This is a path to peace.
  这是一条和平之路。

5. throw： v. 投、扔或掷某物
  Please throw the paper into the wastebasket.
  请把纸扔进废纸篓里。
  Don't throw away yesterday's newspaper.
  别扔昨天的报纸。
  They threw themselves on the food.
  他们扑到了食物上。

6. or： conj. 或，或者；否则
  I'd like it to be black, (or) white, or grey.
  我希望它是黑色的，（或）白色的，或灰色的。
  Put on your overcoat, or you will get a cold.
  穿上你的大衣，不然你会着凉的。

7. until： prep. & conj. 直到……的时候
  She can't leave until Friday.
  直到星期五她才能离开。
  Until he returns, nothing can be done.
  他不回来，什么也做不成。

8. be full to the top with all the snow： 满是积雪，直到房顶
  We climbed to the top of the hill.
  我们爬到了小山顶。
  She is at the top of her profession.
  她正处于她职业的顶峰。

9. somewhere： adv. 某处
  I found my book somewhere in the street.
  我在路上的某个地方找到了我的书。
  My plan has got somewhere.
  我的计划已经取得了一些进展。

10. move： vi. 搬家
  We're going to move next week.
  我们打算下周搬家。

11. Is it far from here?
  离这儿远吗？
  How far is it from here to town?
  从这儿到镇上有多远？
  It seems far from content.
  一点也不满意。

12. pack： v. 打包；n. 包裹，包，捆
  He is packing his belongings.
  他正在打点行李。
  Would you please pack the fruit in jars?
  请你把水果装进罐里好吗？
  Please give me a pack of matches.
  请给我一盒火柴。

13. sure： adv. & adj. 当然
  It sure was a cold night.
  的确是个寒冷的夜晚。
  You are sure to succeed.
  你一定会成功的。
