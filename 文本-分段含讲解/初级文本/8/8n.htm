1. I don't want the food to burn.
  我不想让饭菜烧糊了。
  这句话中的 burn 是主动形式，但带有被动语意，指“饭菜被烧糊”。这种主动表被动的用法
  常用于 want something to do 结构的句子中。另外，want 后跟某人的动词不定式为 want
  somebody to do something。
  The teacher wants the pupils to go home early.
  老师要求学生早点回家。
  Susan doesn't want her daughter to watch television.
  苏珊不让女儿看电视。

2. set the table： 摆桌子
  Would you please set a place for me?
  麻烦你给我安排个地方好吗？

3. put two forks with each plate： 每个盘子配两把叉子
  The car is equipped with snow tires.
  汽车装备了防滑轮胎。

4. look nice： 好看
  That cat looks dangerous.
  那只猫看起来很危险。
  Her face looks like an red apple.
  她的脸看起来像个红苹果。

5. taste good： 好吃
  The medicine tastes bitter.
  这药吃起来很苦。
  It tastes of lemon.
  这东西有柠檬味道。

6. an open fire： 篝火
  a cooking fire：炊火
  It is made with hard fire.
  它是由高温烧成的。

7. remark： v. 说道，谈及
  He remarked that it was getting late.
  他说天色渐晚了。
  He remarked on this subject.
  他就此题目进行了评论。

8. take another bite： 再吃一口
  This peach tastes good; would you want a bite?
  这桃子很好吃，你想来一口吗？
  I haven't had a bite to eat all day.
  我一整天什么也没吃过。
  He might take another route to town.
  他也许走别的路进城了。

9. hot dog： 热狗（西方盛行的一种快餐）

10. wish： v. 希望，但愿，祝福
  He wished her good night.
  他向她道晚安。
  I wish we had a house.
  要是我们有一栋房子多好啊。
  I wish you a very happy future.
  祝你一生幸福。

11. a couple of days： 几天
  I have a couple of things to do.
  我有几件事情要办。
  He waited a couple of hours.
  他等了几个小时。

12. the exposed film： 曝光过的胶卷（即已拍过的）
  This film has been exposed.
  这片子已经感光。

13. develop： v. 冲洗（照片）
  He developed the photographs which he had taken.
  他冲洗了他拍摄的胶卷。

14. Thursday was just two days away.
  到星期四就还有两天。
  The examination was still two weeks away.
  离考试还有两星期。
  The city is miles away.
  这个城市在几英里以外。

15. can / could hardly wait： 简直等不到……
  I could hardly hear the speaker.
  我几乎听不到演说者的声音。
  It was so dark that I could hardly see.
  天那么黑，我简直看不见了。

16. come out： （照片上）显露，洗出
  She came out well in that photograph.
  那张照片中她照得很好。
  Our holiday photos didn't come out because the film was faulty.
  我们假日的照片冲洗不出来，因为胶卷有问题。

17. as soon as： 一……就……
  I will call you as soon as I get back.
  我一回去就给你打电话。
  The stars came out as soon as darkness fell.
  天一黑，星星就出来了。

18. at school： 在学校，在上课，在求学
  Are you at school or at work?
  你上学还是上班？

19. in session： 学校开学，开会，开庭
  the summer session：夏季学期
  have a long session：开长时间的会
  between sessions：休会期间

20. on (one's) vacation： 在（某人的）假期中
  She has gone to Canada on vacation.
  她到加拿大去度假了。
  I worked at my father's store during the college vacation.
  大学放假期间，我在父亲的店里干活。
